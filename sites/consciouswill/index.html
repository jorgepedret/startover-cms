    USE YOUR CONSCIOUS WILL TO MAKE A CONSCIOUS DECLARATION

    Matrix Code CONSWILL.02

    At your Possibility Team, review the Declaring website. Declaring is one of your 3 Powers as a Human Being.

    Sit in groups of 3 with a Possibilitator facing a Client, and a Coach sitting to the side of the Possibiitator.

    The Client's job is to name a person, place, or thing for the Possibilitator to Declare about. Human Beings usually carry around the burden of many undeclared platforms and frameworks due to being disconnected from our Conscious Will. This is an Experiment for the Possibilitator connect to their Conscious Will and make the Declarations.

    The Possibilitator's job is to accept the offer made by the Client, even if the Client chooses the Possibilitator themselves, over and over again, or the Possibilitator's mother or partner, or the Possibilitator's physical body, or the Possibilitator's work!     After 15 minutes, rotate roles and proceed, until everyone has gotten to be in all 3 positions.
