  • Cavitation

    ...the new novel from Clinton Callahan.

    The collapse of modern culture is not the end — it is the opening.
    In the liminal space between what was and what might be, Cavitation weaves myth, method, and possibility into a living blueprint for Archiarchy — a regenerative culture you can taste in your heart and smell in a fresh bright wind.
    This is not just a story you read. It is a story that reads you, calling you to take your place in the emergence of what comes next for human beings on Earth.

    What people are saying:

    Cavitation: the emergence of Archiarchy — the myth and manual of regenerative human culture.

    As the modern world collapses, Cavitation opens the door to what comes next.

    Cavitation: where myth meets method, and possibility becomes practice.

    A book that is a mirror, reflecting what you already know you came here to create.

    Cavitation: the emergence of Archiarchy — the mythopoetic ignition of regenerative human culture.

    FROM THE VIEWPOINT OF A MAGE...

    In times of extreme human-generated chaos, a torus forms.

    Geniuses are drawn together in a torus to learn a new talent. Their efforts catalyze a start-over for human beings.

    That time of collapse is now.

    The torus begins.

    NOTE FROM THE AUTHOR - 3 July 2024

    At this moment, no one else in the world but my Muse and I have read Cavitation... well, more accurately, 'The Muse'... as She is certainly not 'my Muse'. I don't know what She does after hours when She is not involved in coaxing me to let the real stuff come through... But this is not the problem.

    The problem is that I feel jealous of anyone who has not already read Cavitation.

    I am jealous because a new reader gets to experience all of the ecstasies for the first time.

    Seven billion, nine-hundred and ninety-nine million, nine-hundred and ninety-nine thousand, nine-hundred and ninety-eight people have not read this book yet. Try to imagine how much jealousy that is for me to hold.

    No one can yet commiserate with me about this particular affliction.

    Hopefully, soon, you will know what I am talking about. Perhaps there will be many struggling to endure this anguish.

    Then we can start the Sergeant Clinton's Lonely Hearts Club Band... for people who have already enjoyed the 500 pages and are pining away for the sequel... or spontaneously writing a spinoff book themselves!

    But I warn you, it is intense writing stories like these!

    Perhaps there is no other book like this in the world, a book written on the bridges to Archiarchy.

    It could be a new genre of book: the Archan Bridge Books.

    That would make sense, because together we are creating a new genre of culture: Archan Culture!

    I think that the best advice I've heard lately, is, "Don't give me the answer. I want to stay on the Quest!"

    WHAT IS CAVITATION?

    'To cavitate' means 'to make a cave where there was no cave before'.

     

    The procedure for consciously calling forth a new Space where there was already a Space existing is called 'Cavitation' (spelled with a capital 'C' to indicate that this form of Cavitation is done consciously).

     

    Cavitation is a Mage Space Navigation Skill.

     

    Once you learn how to Cavitate new culture Space - or as Gameworld Builders say, Cavitate new 'Gamespace', life will work differently for you. Is this what you want?

    WHY LEARN EVOLUTIONARY SKILLS?

    As with many Possibilitator Skills, you already know how to cavitate new space and have been doing so unconsciously since you were a baby.

    Modern culture claims that awareness does not evolve, and we already know all there is to know.

    It may seem wild to think that the culture and times which currently exist, only exists because so many people keep re-creating it to exist that way each 3 seconds due to their deep-seated unconscious fear that they would not be able to survive if things changed.

    But what if you are already ready to exit Patriarchy? What if you could learn to consciously Cavitate new culture space to come into existence and then inhabit it? What sort of culture would you create?

    Let it be known that in this time of human history, there is a pressing necessity for more Transformation Navigators.

    CAVITATION STUDY GROUP: EXPERIENCING A NEW GENRE OF BOOK

    Cavitation is not science fiction because it is not grounded in future material technological advances, but upgraded nonmaterial technological advances (‘thoughtware upgrade’).

    Cavitation is also not fantasy because the interactions, possibilities and relationships explored in it are not supernatural. They are accessible to anyone who has an interest in growing up.

    Cavitation is a Future Anthropology book, that is, a study of human behavior, cultures, societies, and languages we could use and create in the future. Hopefully, a near future, for our sake.

How could this be happening?

     

    The novel traces a dozen different storylines unfolding in parallel, and taking place in disparate places around the planet. Some of these include a decimated tribal village in Africa, a bombed-out urban neighborhood in the war-torn Middle east, CIA bunkers deep underground, a basement in Eugene, Oregon, even a magical gathering of Hollywood celebrities. As readers, we enter into and follow each storyline. We get to know characters living in widely different circumstances, fleshed out in compelling detail. Little by little, something analogous starts to happen in each different story. A new awareness begins dawning everywhere, new skills, new commitments, new ways of interacting emerge simultaneously. Gradually, these stories start to converge. It’s as if all of it is inspired and coordinated by a larger, evolutionary, planetary force.
