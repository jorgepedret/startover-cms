          You gain your Center, your Feelings, your Voice, your Authority, your Gremlin, your 13 Tools, and your ability to make both Linear Proposals and Transformational Proposals. These and other skills remove you from any necessity to participate in any hierarchy at any level ever again in your life.

          EMOTIONAL REACTIVITY

          NAVIGATING HIGH DRAMA SPACE

          PHOENIXING YOUR TEAM

          GREMLINS IN GAMEWORLDS

          A section about Low drama between participants. Dealing with it from a gameworld culture level and a team-member level, and a spaceholder level.

          WHITE WIDOWS, PSYCHOPATHS, AND NICE BOYS

          Every one of us has developed an effective Survival Strategy - or you would not have survived to your 18th birthday.

          At 18 years old, your Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes should have begun, delivered by the Mages, Initiators, Healers, Trainers, and Adult Women and Men of your archearchy village. This did not happen for you.

          So... you are where are, and that is where your Possibilitator Path begins.

          Nonetheless, you are trapped inside of your Survival Strategy until you build enough Matrix to Radically Rely on your Bright Principles to empower your Archetypal Lineage to do its work in the world through you.

          After you make some 'baby steps' and the world starts working differently for you, you suddenly become more attractive to those creatures who skulk about, feeding off of other people's good fortune, the vampires and zombies...
