    • Conscious Speaking

      SPEAKINGS. 1. Normal Neurotic Speaking, 2. Discussion (either Neurotic or Adult), 3. Adult Speaking, 4. Possibility Speaking, 5. Discovery Speaking, 6. Dragon Speaking, 7. Speaking from the Unknown, 8. Transformational Speaking

      1. NORMAL NEUROTIC SPEAKING

      Normal Neurotic Speaking is the most common sort of speaking on Earth. Its purpose is not about communicating... more like commiserating, being miserable together. Often both people speak at the same time, so only the illusion of listening happens. These days, most people have their phone in hand, scanning for temporary adrenalin hits while moving their mouth. Normal Neurotic Speaking is more about pretending to not be lonely, while simultaneously being terrified of authentic intimacy.

      2. DISCUSSION (WHICH IS EITHER NORMAL NEUROTIC SPEAKING OR ADULT SPEAKING)

      Discussion in a group can arise as either Normal Neurotic Speaking (gossip, power struggles, striving for high status) or Adult Speaking (information transfer, Collaborative Speaking, Creative Collaboration, Teamwork, Invention, Toroidal Decision Making). Have you ever been to meetings where even before it starts you already know it will be a waste of time? The 'boss' has already made the decisions and could have sent it out in a memo instead of running the charade. It is up to you to transform a sham meeting through putting the poop on the table, pulling the rug out, or causing a Phoenix Process or a M.E.S.S. Process, or to decide to not participate.

      3. ADULT SPEAKING

      Adult Level Responsibility is fair. The idea behind adult level responsibility is: "I will do my share, if you do your share." In modern culture, engaging other people in a context of Adult Level Responsibility is already extraordinary, because modern culture is solidly centered on Child Level Responsibility (making huge messes with no intention at all of ever cleaning them up). To find Adult Level Responsibility and Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes, you would need to go to the edge of modern culture's thoughtware and either remain at the edge, or take one step over the edge, and stay there. Adult Speaking has its origin on the Adulthood evolutionary Path. If a person is not on the Path, then their speaking comes more from the Parent Egostate, with its opinions of right and wrong, its beliefs, its rules, etc., and it is not authentic Adult Speaking.

      4. POSSIBILITY SPEAKING

      Humans have the capacity to be the space through which archetypal forces can do their work in the world. Huge resources become available through a person who has learned to be Centered, Grounded, and Bubbled, and who can Cavitate Space so that the Bright Principle of Possibility can bring its treasures into the world. The resource of Possibility Speaking does not come from the speaker. It comes through the speaker. Since the Bright Principle of Possibility has neither hands nor feet, eyes nor mouth, it cannot become conscious and practical except through a Possibility Speaker. In gratitude, the Bright Principle of Possibility gives you ten percent as it comes through you. This you have to experience in order to understand.

      5. DISCOVERY SPEAKING

      Discovery Speaking is a group expedition into new territory. It starts with a Spaceholder who Cavitates the Discovery Space, a timekeeper, a documenter, and a Quest. The Quest needs to have Integrity, and it needs to come from authentic Necessity. Discovery Speaking serves a consciously stated Purpose, which is: to Discover something. Discovery Speaking works in parallel with Discover Listening. You can detect when your Discovery Speaking is working because you will sense the 'yellow stuff' vibrational humming energy filling the space from the Bright Principle of Discovery. You will also be discovering new Distinctions and Possibilities.

      6. DRAGON SPEAKING

      Dragon Speaking is when you unleash the Clarity of Conscious Anger, Conscious Fear, Conscious Sadness, and Conscious Joy all at the same time. Needless to say... Dragon Speaking is LOUD. If it is not LOUD, it is not Dragon Speaking. Each person has a Dragon living in their Being. You have probably suppressed your Dragon from Speaking all your life because it can be disruptive, especially in a positive sense. It can interfere with sleep, indolence, lassitude, sloppiness, corruption, deception, and Gremlin feeding. Healing yourself through Emotional Healing Processes (EHP) - especially Past Life Vows - and training yourself through Heart Gym, Rage Club, Expand The Box training, and Possibility Labs prepares your Dragon to awaken and let itself be known. When this happens, it is a celebration for the whole village!

      7. SPEAKING FROM THE UNKNOWN

      Modern culture fears the unknown by making its second highest value 'knowing'. Modern culture regards not knowing as a handicap, a faux pas, almost a sin. In, Archiarchy, on the other hand, we have discovered that the Unknown is a vast - actually an Infinite Resource - that can be tapped by a Possibilitator through proper training and practice. Speaking from the Unknown becomes particularly valuable when answering perplexing or complicated questions, or when solving 'wicked problems'. Speaking from the Unknown is amplified when the Speaker can speak into vacuum, such as a resistance-free Possibility Listening space held by a conscious Listener.

      8. TRANSFORMATIONAL SPEAKING

      Transformational Speaking is one of the greatest human potentials, and sadly so underrecognized. Transformational Speaking occurs within, and actually helps to invoke, the Archetypal Domains.

      Transformational Speaking is so underused probably because modern culture is contexted in Child Level Responsibility, and Transformational Speaking becomes operational only in the Adult Egostate where you use your own voice as yourself in the Minimized NOW of the Present.

      Transformational Speaking is known as a 'speech act', the act of creating something real by speaking. Another way to say this is 'Abrah Kadehbra' which roughly translates as, "I create as I speak." For Transformational Speaking to actually cause something in reality, the speaker would need to Take A Stand in a context of Integrity and Accountability. Such personal healing and personal agency development occurs during Possibilitator Training.

      Below we list 10 different forms of Transformational Speaking:

      1. Metaconversation (Having a conversation about the conversation creates the possibility of Possibility.)
      2. Making A Promise (Having Integrity means doing what you say you will do.)
      3. Taking A Stand
      4. Cavitating Space
      5. Making a Declaration (for example, "I am changing my mind," or, "I decide that...")
      6. Making a Commitment
      7. Making Proposals
      8. Making Transformational Proposals
      9. Making Negotiations
      10. Asking a Question for which the answer exists in a different space. In other words, using Questions for Navigating Space and Creating Possibility.

      Cookies are bits of information (memory files) stored in your browser by sites you visit. As is common practice with almost all websites, this site uses cookie to improve your experience by remembering your preferences and enable other cookie-based features (e.g. analytics).

      Our Cookies

      When you submit data through a form such as those found on contact pages or comment forms, cookies may be set to remember your user details for future correspondence.

      In order to provide you with a better experience and remember your preferences, we need to set cookies so that this information can be called whenever you interact with a page.

      Third-Party cookies

      In special cases, we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties like Google Analytics. Third party analytics are used to track and measure usage of this site so that we can continue to produce engaging content.

      How To Disable Cookies?

      Most browsers let you to refuse to accept cookies. See your browser Help or Tool for how to do this. Be aware that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and many other websites you visit. Therefore, it is recommended that you do not disable cookies.

      Cookie Use
      We use cookies to ensure a smooth browsing experience. By continuing we assume you accept the use of cookies.
