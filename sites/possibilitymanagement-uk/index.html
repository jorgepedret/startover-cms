        in the UK

        Real Transformation for a Purpose Driven Life

        I'M READY!

      • "The opposite of scarcity is not Abundance, it's Creation."

                                                                           Clinton Callahan

      • BRIDGING POSSIBILITIES

        Modern Initiation into Adulthood

        Possibility Management builds bridges between modern culture which brings humanity to its limits, and the next cultures which are sustainable and sane.

         

        Possibility Management is upgraded thoughtware for catalyzing change in the physical, intellectual, emotional or energetic obstacles blocking you and your team from using non-obvious possibilities. It works by converting inner barriers into fertilizer, doorways, rocket ships, or avoidable mud puddles along your path of evolution.

         

        In more than 40 years of empirical research, Possibility Management has gained a wealth of distinctions about feelings and emotions and now offers cutting-edge practices and tools for healing and empowering – on a personal and thus global level. We are looking forward to bringing them to the UK.

      • Expand the Box Training

      • A training in the context of Possibility Management is

        modern initiation work - a real experience of change.

         

        Expand The Box is a safe learning environment where you work as a team to enter new territory and discover hidden fields of human possibility. The key to these discoveries lies in the extraordinary thoughtmaps, tools, techniques and processes of Possibility Management.

        We train individually, in pairs, in small groups and as a whole group. Learning occurs through practicing new forms of experiencing and creating in the safest of conditions,

        where you can make maximum mistakes with only positive consequences.

         

        What if…
        … instead of merely functioning one day after the other you lived your life intensely and purposefully?
        ...instead of wallowing in your perceived impotence at making any difference you discovered a playful liveliness and your empowered, authentic, unique self that loves life and being alive?
        … instead of being at the mercy of your emotions you used your feelings as valuable signposts to navigate everyday life?
        … instead of feeling alone and desolate you experienced a deep and wondrous connection to all living things and life itself?
        … instead of being afraid from the potential for destruction when standing in your own power you felt awed by your creative potential?
        … instead of helplessly hoping for things to change you felt empowered to take radical responsibility for creating the world your heart knows is possible?

         

         

        It is not school, and it is not a seminar.

        Expand The Box is a paradigm shift. It’s the core training for Possibility Management where you reinvent who you are, ​where you learn things you were not taught in school.

        It leads to a beneficial change of perspective and an expanded perception so that you live your vision rather than dreaming about it.

         

         

        You will work with your physical, intellectual, emotional, and your energetic bodies.

        You will tap inner and outer resources, which go far beyond what (just) the mind could ever provide you with. You pave the way to become your destiny in action.

         

         

        Expand The Box is a possibility to step into full responsibility of your life, to upgrade your value and belief system and learn new tools to take a big step into a more vivid and purpose-driven way of living.
