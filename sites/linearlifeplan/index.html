    WHAT IS A LIFE PLAN?

    The term 'Life Plan' refers to the Gameplan for your own personal life.

     

    A Gameplan is the sum of your strategy, your aims, your path, and the qualities desired or valued in your life.

     

    This does not mean that mere survival is not a Life Plan. On the contrary, it is perhaps the most common human Life Plan on Earth.

     

    Does Survival have to be the most common Life Plan of humans on Earth?

     

    No, it does not.

     

    Think of what would be different if most humans had more on their minds and hearts than where they will get their next hamburger or bowl of rice.

     

    Starting a conversation about Life Plans opens up questions you may never have considered answering before, such as:

    • What is/was my parent's Life Plan?
    • Who actually chooses my Life Plan?
    • If I have unconsciously adopted my current Life Plan, where did it come from?
    • Where would I learn about or see examples of different Life Plans?
    • What Life Plan would benefit me the most?
    • Which 'me' is asking about 'benefits'?
    • Can I be arrested, or put in jail, or even killed for having a Life Plan that differs from the social norms or status quo?
    • Has that happened to me before in Past Lives?

    Unless a Life Plan is disturbed, it tends to continue along its current trajectory until the Life Plan user is dead.

     

    And, Life Plans typically only evolve between lives. This suggests that once a Life Plan has been invoked, it tends to persist along its original trajectory until it reaches its end. The evolutionary forces of realization, recognition, regrets, awareness increase, or conscious remorse, only have their effect on the deathbed when it is too late to change anything about your current life.

    CRITICALLY PERTURBING YOUR LIFE PLAN

    A Life Plan is what Ilya Prigogine defined as a 'Dissipative Structure', that is, a structure which is sustained by the energy that flows through it. A fine example of a Dissipative Structure is a tornado. Clearly the tornado exists. You can see the trail of chaos and destruction it weaves along its path. But the tornado itself is created out of the wind energy that flows through it. As soon as the wind energy stops, the tornado is gone, vanished into thin air.

     

    A Life Plan is similar to a tornado, in that, as soon as there is no more life energy flowing through a Life Plan, the Life Plan itself vanishes into thin air.

     

    Who is putting the life energy into your Life Plan? You are.

     

    It that a conscious choice or an unconscious choice? We will soon see.

     

    What happens after you remove your life energy from a Life Plan? The Life Plan, in your case, disappears. But that would, in a sense mean that you would have to die for your Life Plan to die. It would also mean that even if you die and your Life Plan dies, that particular Life Plan could still exist in the Morphogenetic Field of the human race because others still use it, meaning that the design of the Life Plan would still shape the culture that others are born into. This makes for a not so interesting conversation...

     

    However, Dissipative Structures have a characteristic possibility of 'reordering' into a higher level elegance, awareness, sophistaction, and beauty if they are 'critically perturbed', which means, if they are disturbed to a precise degree or in a particular fashion.

     

    If you 'Under Perturb' a Life Plan , it has a certain 'Box-like' quality of resilience that it will recover its original shape and purpose to the degree that no perturbation remains.

     

    If you 'Over Perturb' a Life Plan , it has the tendency to completely disappear, being crushed or blown away by the intensity of the disturbance.

     

    However, if you 'Critically Perturb' a Life Plan - which means to disturb it within a certain window of intensity - not too much and not too little - then the system 'reorders' to qualities of being more resilient or intelligent.

     

    The purpose of this website, and the purpose of examining your particular Life Plan, is to consider the possibility of 'Critically Perturbing' your Life Plan to free you into higher orders of life expression and experience!
