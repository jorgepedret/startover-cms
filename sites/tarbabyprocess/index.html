        • The informal term 'tar baby' refers to: 'a difficult problem that is made worse by attempts to solve it'. Groups and couples often suffocate in an emotional 'tar baby'.

          The 'Tar Baby Process' can transform this 'tar' into evolutionary gold.

          The Tar-Baby is the second of the Uncle Remus stories published in 1881 by Joel Chandler Harris, and popularized in Song of the South, the 1946 Disney movie. Other versions of the story, however, have surfaced throughout the world, including Nigeria, Brazil, Corsica, Jamaica, India, and the Philippines. The folk-tale is about a decoy doll made of tar and turpentine used by the villainous Br'er Fox to entrap Br'er Rabbit. The more that Br'er Rabbit fights the Tar-Baby, the more entangled he becomes.

        • Background - looking at what is under the carpet

          Sticky social conflicts that seem to persist or even expand no matter how hard you earnestly try to rid yourself of them seem to plague human good heartedness.

           

          This is a 'Tar Baby' problem.

           

          Tar Baby problems can be solved using the Tar Baby Process, one of the Emotional Healing Processes from Torus Technology, powered by Possibility Management.

        • Foreground - diving into the tar

          The catalytic transformational force of conscious awareness about what is really going on dissolves the tar.

          The more you try to explain or escape from the problem, the more you seem to get stuck in it.

           

          You are about to give up hope.

           

          The Tar is 'Emotional Charge', unexpressed emotions being denied.

           

          We will us 5 Body Scanning to discover what is really going on, both within individuals, and between individuals. We will also scan for confusions in the Gameworld, in terms of unclear Purpose or Mixed Contexts.

           

        • How To Do The Tar Baby Process

          Arrange for a Space, a Spaceholder, a Circle of Chairs (not sitting on the floor), and a Documenter To Make Notes.

          Also have at hand boxes of tissues, a few hand towels (Rage Cloths), a few plastic barf buckets.
          1

          SET CONTEXT

          Before delivering the Tar Baby Process, be sure that the agreement is to be standing in a Context of Radical Responsibility, 3 Phase Healing, and the Bright Principle of Transformation. If these elements are missing from the relationship or from the Gameworld, you are in unstable ground and will likely be attacked by uninitiated Gremlins and banished from the Space. The Tar Baby Process removes large sources of Gremlin Food, and the uninitiated Gremlins might oppose this.

          2

          SCAN YOURSELVES

          Spaceholder keeps their Archetypal Sword Of Clarity to hand (sharp at both ends) and keeps it pointed at each person's neck.

          The Distinctions about Gremlin should be landed in the Space at the start of the Tar Baby Process.

          Ask each person to Scan themselves for emotional charge and explains what they feel angry, sad, glad, or scared about and why.

          3

          BE SCANNED BY THE SPACEHOLDER

          Next the Spaceholder 5 Body Scans each person in the group for Emotional Charge and inquires about what is going on there, making a safe place for each person to feel and express their emotions.

          4

          SCAN EACH OTHER

          Next have the members Scan each other for Emotional Charge that is blocking them from being with each other.

          In each case, identify the emotion and the incident that happened at the moment it started.

          Put it all on the table.

          5

          IDENTIFY THE 'EMOTIONAL TAR'

          Choose one or two people in the group to explain to everyone what is going on for them.

          • What happened that covers over the connections with 'Emotional Tar'? 
          • What is interfering with connections and keeping interactions to a superficial inauthentic level?
          6

          IDENTIFY 'EMOTIONAL TAR' GENERATING AGENTS

          This can take a significant amount of time in the meeting because you are probably landing new Distinctions in the Space. People need to have the Matrix to hold and respect the Distinctions. This part of the Process needs to be done formally and it must be documented for all to see and consider on a Flip Chart board and in Beep! Books. Discovering each of these agents is the same as discoving a Doorway to an Emotional Healing Process for the people. Be sure they write it down and commit do doing the Emotional Healing Process. This is not a Low Drama Circus show. This is about growing up into Radical Responsibility.

          Spaceholder helps each person to identify:

          • Assumptions.
          • Expectations.
          • Conclusions.
          • Resentments.
          • Stories.
          • Projections. 
          • Fears.
          • Incomplete Emotional Healing Processes (EHP). NOTE: If an emotional Reactivity is still active, the Process is NOT complete. A complete Process As-Ises the Reactivity and it no longer exists. Processes have layers, just as human beings have Parts. It is worth it to through all the layers. 
          • Confusions (unconscious or sideways anger).
          • Reactivity (Buttons, Hooks, Triggers, Traumas, Imbalances, Voices).
          • Gremlin Contamination in the Adult Ego State.
          7

          SHARE AWARENESS

          Make sure everyone is seen in their world.

          Make sure everyone is heard in their pain.

          This is a 3 Phase Healing process. It Causes Transformation.

          Shared awareness makes the 'emotional tar' go away. Notice the 'tar' dissolving. Drop things in Black Holes if needed.

          8

          RENEGOTIATE INTIMACY

          What will each person do differently next time? Renegotiate what works and what does not work starting now.

          Be clear and specific.

          Make new agreements.

          Define new Experiments that each person will try.
