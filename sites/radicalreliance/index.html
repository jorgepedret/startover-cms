  • Radical Reliance?

    Ordinarily you rely on what other people rely on. Then it is not your fault if it does not work.

    The back door is directly behind you, because you can blame the others. You can blame the system.

    What is Radical Reliance?

    Radical Reliance is taking Radical Responsibility for choosing what you rely upon, and then relying on it.

    What you rely upon as a foundation - the references you defer to - determines what you can create.

     

    If you want to create different results, change what you rely upon.

     

    Without questioning it, you assume that the best things to rely upon are the current context of society, the beliefs of your parents, and what is advertised on television and the media. These have worked so far, haven't they? What harm could it do to keep relying on them?

     

    This question has suddenly become exceptionally relevant. The harm has revealed itself. Life on planet Earth is being exterminated, 200 species per day.

     

    After taking a look around it becomes immediately clear that these are not viable platforms on which to stand. Other resources are available. What are you going to do about it?

    RADICAL RELIANCE IS NOT BLIND TRUST

    Blind trust can be fatal, holding the fantasy that you can fly if you believe you can fly, and then taking a step off the side of a building.

     

    Blind trust is crossing your fingers, hoping that a delusion will become real. This is naive and unreliable.

     

    Why do so many people think they have no other alternative?

     

    It is straightforward to gain and practice the skills of reinventing life foundations so that they promote a different outcome.

     

    First, of course, you must think of the idea of reinventing life platforms.

     

    You have been taught to conform and to submit to what already exists - even if it does not work - rather than to step sideways through previously unnoticed Doorways into new resources out of which to invent entirely new platforms for human lives and culture.

     

    That is one purpose why we built this website for you: to offer you the novel idea that you can discover exactly how to rebuild the foundational platforms in your life, and learn how to stand on these new platforms to generate whole new life and relationship and economic opportunities. Reinventing platforms is one way to manage possibility.

     

    Previous to now you may have thought you much choose between gullible blind trust, or pessimism.

     

    Is this why the majority of modern culture news and popular sentiment is so cynical? Is this why voters in a democratic government would elect unfavorable characters to office over and over, rather than reinventing the institution to which they submit themselves for governance?

     

    Is this the best we can do? I don't think so...

     

    The next transformation starts with you.
