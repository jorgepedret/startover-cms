        EXPAND THE BOX

        EXPAND THE BOX is a paradigm shift. It is a Training where you reinvent who you are, where you learn things you were not taught in school.
         

        ETB is the core training for Possibility Management: a 3 day safe and astonishing learning environment for upgrading traditional thinking and behaviors.
         

        In a guided space, you gradually leave behind beliefs, dependencies and thought patterns that you unconsciously adopted from others or from your childhood. You start discovering new territories of human possibility and unfolding the rich potential of you own life.
         

        The key to these discoveries lies in the extraordinary Thoughtmaps, Tools and Processes of the context of Possibility Management.


        APPLICATION AND INFORMATION:

        POSSIBILITY LAB

        Completing Expand The Box qualifies you to attend a POSSIBILITY LAB: a 5 day training to be fully empowered as a skilled change-agent,
        less in theory and more in practice.

        Possibility Labs are brilliantly clear and profoundly safe spaces
        for transformation and healing.

         

        POSSIBILITY LABS INITIATE YOU INTO RESPONSIBILITY FOR YOUR:
         

        Center • Circumstances • Level of responsibility • Happiness • Thoughts • Feelings • Time • Space • Energy • Health • Attention • Stories • Money • Choices • Purpose • Possibility • Tone of Voice • Vocabulary •

        And for • The Culture you live in • The greater community.


        IN A POSSIBILITY LAB, YOU LEARN TO:
         

        • Use your feelings for handling things and your emotions for healing things

        • Dissolve inner blocks by transforming restrictive beliefs and old decisions

        • Heal emotional and energetic wounds & re-arrange relational agreements

        • Realize that the problem is not the situation itself, but its interpretation

        • Create clarity and make boundaries

        • Create opportunities rather than waiting for them

        • Communicate fully by using your voice and body language

        • Live authentic and deep relationships

        • Function with ease in the center of chaos
