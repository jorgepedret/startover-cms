Welcome to the Tui Spiritual & Educational Trust

The healthy social life is found when in the mirror of each human soul the whole community finds its reflection and when in the community the strength of each individual is living. - Rudolf Steiner

The Tui Spiritual & Educational Trust is a charitable Trust dedicated to the general promotion and enhancement of community life.

The Trust gains much of its impetus from the working example of Tui Community and some of its objectives are realised by providing programmes in which people may visit, experience and learn the workings of an intentional community.

The Trust is also passionate about providing Events for Adults, Teenagers and Children.

Tui Community was founded in 1984. Twenty years later it is a blend of an “intentional community”, and a “village” where people have deliberately come together to share lives in a way that goes beyond nuclear family living.

Today 30 – 40 adults and children live in a beautiful place by the sea, Wainui Bay, on the edge of the Abel Tasman National Park in New Zealand’s South Island.

Among the many activities of the community, we grow a large organic garden, an integrated organic orchard, and some shared communal meals.
