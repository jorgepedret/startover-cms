      • Kinds Of Declaring

        All Declaring uses Is Glue.

        Each kind of Declaring serves a particular Shadow Principle.

        BELIEFS

        The purpose of Beliefs is to 'know the truth' so your Box can deny the unknown, or avoid being embarrassed by having to say, "I don't know."

        CONCLUSIONS

        The purpose of Conclusions is to try to solidify the Universe by making it rigid, completed, and known by you. Then your Box can avoid evolution.

        ASSUMPTIONS

        The purpose of Assumptions is to create a fantasy world that your Box derives comforts from living inside. Then you can avoid reality.

        EXPECTATIONS

        The purpose of Expectations is to solidify your Assumptions into a force that controls people around you to match your Box's shape so you can avoid fear.

        POSITIONALITY

        The purpose of Positionality is to disconnect your Being from reality and from evolution to isolate your Box in a fantasy world outside of relationship.

        PREJUDICES

        The purpose of Prejudice is to make yourself 'right' and 'superior', to pretend as if you already 'know', and to avoid anything that shocks your Box.

        PROJECTIONS

        The purpose of Projections is to force your environment to conform to your Box habits so that you can use your familiar survival patterns.

        STORY WORLD

        The purpose of Story Worlds is to inhabit a small enough part of the Universe that your Box can find 'friends' who inhabit your same Story World.

        JUDGEMENTS

        Judgements are internal Declarations to yourself how 'bad' or 'good' you are. The purpose of harsh Judgements is to kill yourself so no one else has to kill you. The purpose of self-praise Judgements is to build a Fantasy World. There is something suicidal about both kinds of Judgements.

        RESENTMENT

        Resentment is a 5-Body Reactivity that Box and Gremlin activate to close down intimacy. Why block intimacy? Because learning and transformation occur through Intimacy. Relationships do not die from a lack of love. Relationships die from a lack of Intimacy.
