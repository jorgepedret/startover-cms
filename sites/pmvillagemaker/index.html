        Villaging Does Not Exist In Modern Culture

        Modern culture citizens live in cities.

         

        Patriarchal institutions design the physical, energetic, emotional and intellectual environment of the city in order to fulfill the purpose of ever-increasing material value production and profit. City-dwellers sacrifice their ability to feel, choose, ask, declare in exchange for 'money to live'. Cities are breeding grounds for Zombism.

         

        The morphogenetic field of cities is saturated with already obsolete and old patriarchal, capitalist and colonialist thoughtware.

         

        Villaging happens beyond the edge of Modern Culture.

         

        Archan Villaging is Edgework.

         

        New Age is saturated with the mythology of 'remembering what we have forgotten' from the times human beings lived in indegenious tribe roaming the Earth. But, the child-level-responsibility (centered on the thoughtware: 'Mother Earth will take care of us') green-brain matriarchal Villaging technologies does not have the Evolutionary wish and Memetic structure to nurture the radiant force of your unique Archetypal Lineage in action.

         

        Archiarchy has never existed on Earth before. It is the natural evolution of humanity as a whole from Baby culture (Matriarchal, if they ever existed) to Adolescent culture (Patriarchy) to Authentic Adulthood culture.

         

        When trying to fit into the adolescent-level-responsibility hierarchical warrior-clan competitive modern culture Villaging strategies, you are forced to appear dead and childish, which you are not.

         

        Archan Villaging is exiting survivalism and centering your interactions on Living in the company of other Path-walkers.

         

        Archiarchy Villaging creates Gaian Growness Gameworlds which you bring to life by collaborating so that you win, so that the others who are Villaging win, and so that the elegance, beauty, and evolutionary sophistication of 'the Village' itself wins (including Gaia...) in a mutually-empowering Torus, circulating around Edgeworkers who deliver their Nonmaterial Value through 3 Phase Healing, Thoughtware Upgrade, and Adult and Archetypal Initiatory Processes.

         

        Village-Making is Taking a Stand for an entire morphogenetic field of interaction and creation to upgrade to an Adult (or Radical) level of Responsibility.

        People come together and 'village'. This means that 'to Village' is a verb, not a noun.

        A 'Village' arises through 'Villaging'.

         

        There is also no such thing as 'a village'. The visible and invisible elements of Villaging create an ever-evolving 'cyclone' of people helping each other live full-out, heal, grow up, and bring each person's potentials to life.

         

        There is no such thing as 'a villager', because without other villagers there is no Village.

         

        Villaging requires an entire new set of sentivity, awarenesses and skills that can really only be learned by Villaging itself.

         

        Much like Village-Making, which is practiced by being the source of Villaging happening.

        42 Actions For Successful Archan Village-Making

        Successful Village-Makers have their Team engaged simultenously in at least 80% of these 42 Archan Village-Making actions at all times. These actions participate in inhabiting the framework that establish Archiarchy as the culture of your Villaging.

        1. DEPEEN THE CONTEXT: If you are not ongoingly investigating, expanding and upgrading the context of your Villaging, it will crystalizes into a fanatical rule-based belief-oriented religion context. One characteristic of Archan culture is the ability to subvert itself. In Archan Villaging, members are Homo Evolutio - the ones who evolve, and no longer Homo Sapiens - the one who 'know'. Deepening the context of Villaging is living in the Quest of: "How could this Villaging go better? In which territory can the Villaging expand into? What part of the Villaging needs clarifying?" Context deepening happens mostly during the 3Cell Village-Making meetings. The Evolutions are then spread into through Villaging in Articles, Websites, Newsletters, Announcements (in the Telegram groups), different kind of feedbacks to the Villaging members, in the weekly Heartbeat Villaging call and by keeping the Codex up-to-date.
        2. WRITE AND UPGRADE THE CODEX: the Guardians are responsible for the upkeep of the Villaging Codex. The Codex is a PUBLIC document that anyone who is interested to join the Villaging MUST read BEFORE entering the Village, and agree to it. The Codex can be found on the Telegram group (pinned message) and on the main websited. For examples of Codexes, please go to: https://gameworldbuilder.mystrikingly.com/#codex.
        3. DIVERSIFY THE RULES OF ENGAGEMENT: The strenght of your Villaging is not the elegance Rules of Engagement, it is the Clarity and Depth of your Context. The Rules of Engagement clarify the negotiable parts of the gameworld (in constrast, the context is non-negotiable). For example, how does someone enter the gameworld? how does someone leave the gameworld? what are the exchange of currencies within the Village? and with other gameworld? The Rules of the Engagement can also be used a stepping stones to integrate new awarenesses and therefore, new behaviours, into the Context. The Rules of Engagmenent are specific agreements that answers the question: "How does it go?" "What agreements empower the purpose of the Village?" Designing Rules of Engagement requires the intelligence of all four Archetypal Lineage categories.

How can the new information travel from the Infinity Ring (3Cell Village-Making Team) to the Village, and the other way too? What we discovered is that a weekly meeting held by the Village-Maker or attended by the Village-Maker is an ideal place. It becomes the Heart (or one of the Hearts) of the Village. The purpose of the space is Transformation, Thoughtware Upgrade, Healing and Initiation such as a Study Group, a Possibility Team or a Clinic. The Heart Beat of the Village is a space for Aliveness, which would include sharings authentic Liquid States, clear and sharp Feedback, Discovery journey following an authentic question from someone in the space, laying out newly discovered discernment, tools, Maps and Processes for the benefit of the whole Village. These meetings are recorded and publicly shared.
