        • Where The Possibilitator Training Tribe Comes Together

          SLACK: Possibilitator Training Global

          https://trainerpath.slack.com/

          The Possibilitator Training Slack is the main Hub for the Global Possibilitator Training. There you can find:

          • #profile: each Possibilitator's profile
          • #general: for celebrations, success, questions, sharings...
          • #emotional healing process: to ask and receive
          • the different projects that are happening and cross pollinating in Possibilitator Training Global

          TELEGRAM: Possibilitator Training Regional

          Each Possibilitator Training Regional figures out the best way for them to be connected. The favored option (as of now) is to use a Telegram group.

          EMAIL: Possibilitator Training Newsletter

          Every once in a while, a Possibilitator Training Newsletter is sent.
          The Possibilitator Training Newsletter announces upcoming Possibilitator Training  Labs, special projects and announcements, and other meeting and training Spaces.

        • Possibilitator Training Events Calendars

          GOOGLE CALENDAR: Internal Offers

          Visible & Accessible only to the Possibilitator Training Tribe

          The Google Calendar is only for Internal Offers, meaning only open for those in Possibilitator Training, including Regional weekly calls, Possibilitator Training Gathering, Women's Circle, Men's Circle...

           

          For access to the Possibilitator Training calendar, you need to apply and be interviewed and accepted into Possibilitator Training first. If you are in Possibilitator Training and you do not yet have access to this calendar, please contact Anne-Chloé.

          TEAM UP CALENDAR: External Offers

          Visible to the world & Accessible only to the Possibilitator Training Tribe

          Team Up is the Calendar for all Online Possibility Management contexted offers, including Possibility Teams, Rage Clubs, Fear Clubs, Gremlin Clubs, Unmix Your Feelings Clubs, S.P.A.R.K. Experiment Teams, Study Groups, WorkTalks, Workshops, etc.
          Most of the offers are made by those in Possibilitator Training, by TTTS (Trainer-Trainer Training School), or Trainer Guild Trainers. Sometimes, a Possibilitator might add events offered by other Possibility Managers.

          The link to access the 'read only' Calendar: https://teamup.com/ksv2xq34sxiagcuei1

          To modify or add an event on the Team Up Calendar, the link is in the #calendar thread in Possibilitator Training Slack.

          FACEBOOK PM PAGE: External Offers

          Visible for the world & Accessible only to Editors

          The Facebook Possibility Management page is administrated by Anne-Chloé Destremau and is edited by Nicole Bradford, Vera Franco, Joana Cruz, Clinton Callahan and Brianne Vaillancourt.

          If you want you Possibility Management events to be shared on the FB PM Page, please reach out to one of us.
          https://www.facebook.com/possibilitymanagement/

        • Where To Find Thoughtmaps, Processes,

          & Possibilitator Training Gameworld Documents

          PM Thoughtmaps

          https://pmthoughtmaps.mystrikingly.com/

          Find the Possibility Management Thoughtmaps here.
          As a Possibility Management Trainers, regularly go through the PM Thoughtmaps website and discover thoughtmaps that you did not know before. Learn to deliver them. By delivering them, they will land in your 5 bodies.

          PM Processes

          https://pmprocesses.mystrikingly.com/

          Find the Possibility Management Processes that have been written up. Most of the Possibility Management Processes have not been written up and therefore will not available for the next generations of Possibilitators and Possibility Management Trainers to come.

          Part of your Trainer Path is to write up the processes that you have discovered and/or processes that have not yet been written up, taking a stand for the next generations of Possibility Management Trainers.

          Please write to Anne-Chloé about the format for writing up PM Processes.

          PM Archive

          In Google Drive

          The Possibility Management Archive is divided in 5 sections:

          1. Trainer Team
          2. ETB
          3. PLAB
          4. Trainer Path
          5. Trainer Trainer
          You have access to different sections depending on the Trainings that you are delivering. A more details structure plan is available here in PDF. Contact Anne-Chloé for access to the different sections of the Drive.
