      • What's 'Is-Glue'?

        A tube of Is-Glue is one of the 13 Tools on a

        Possibility Manager's Toolbelt.

         

        Is-Glue is a special kind of glue, very popular with humans. Actually each of us was born with a tube of Is-Glue on our waist and we use it all day every day. Is-Glue is a story-making device.

         

        Human beings are story makers.

         

        To make a Story, we take an object (like he or she, or my life, or his life, or this website, or my dinner, or anything!) and we glue it together with an opinion, a judgment, a conclusion, a decision ("He is hot." Or, "She is always blaming me." Or, "My life is not like I wish it was." Or, "My life is fabulous." Or, "My life is pointless," etc... ) with Is-Glue.

         

        The thing is that 'is' does not exist in nature.

        'Is' is a Construct invented by the human Mind.

        (There are human languages that do not use the word 'is.')

         

        You ongoingly and perhaps, until this very moment,

        use Is-Glue to serve the Unconscious Purposes of your Box and

        your Gremlin by Declaring that two completely disassociated

        concepts are stuck together as a Story.

         

        Then you step into your Story World and act as if your Story is true,

        even though just a moment before you made it up

        out of nothing using Is-Glue.

         

        For example: “This job is impossible.” 

        Or “My team loves doing impossible jobs.”

         

        You can change your mind about everything. That is what minds are for, to be changed. It is your mind after all.

        You can do whatever you want with it.

         

        (If you want to change your mind,

        Is-Glue Dissolver is the tool of the day!)

        Experiments

        MAKE A VIDEO OF THE IS CONJUGATIONS

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.01

        You are using Is-Glue hundreds, if not thousands of times a day to create the stories that make up the reality that you live within.

         

        Usually this story making is unconscious i.e. you don't even realise that you are doing it. Meaning you do not know what the purpose of your Is-Glue creations really are. This means that most likely many of them will be serving Low Drama and Shadow Principles like Revenge, Competition, Scarcity etc

         

        Noticing you are using Is-Glue is the beginning of using it consciously.

         

        This experiment builds capacity for this.

         

        First, in your Beep! Book, write and memorize a list of conjugations of "is":

         

        is, am, are, was, were, has, have, had, do, does, did, may, might, must, can, could, should, would, shall, will and the "not" forms

         

        We call these "Is-Glue Formulations" or "Is-Glue Conjunctions".

         

        Next, make a short video about you saying them, with a dance, without reading them.

         

        Do it using each of the Four Feelings: anger, sadness, fear and joy. Choose many different ways to say them.

         

        When you have made the video, please enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.01 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

         

        This Experiment is worth 1 Matrix Point.

        SKIP THE "IS"ES

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.03

        This experiment IS about spotting the Is-Glue before you even use it.

         

        For one whole day, do not use the word IS or one of the conjugations of the word IS.

         

        For example, instead of saying "We are going to the store", leave out the "are" and say: "We going to the store."

         

        Every time you use one by accident (Beep!), eat a frozen Brussel sprout (Shift!) and keep going (Go!) to empower Rapid Learning.

         

        This is not about beating yourself up for making mistakes. Be on the Learning Team!

         

        After a whole day, write in your Beep! Book for at least 20 minutes about how things went.

         

        Then, no matter how many Brussel Sprouts you had to or didn't have to eat, enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.03 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

        NOTICE STORIES ABOUT STORIES

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.06

        There can be many layers of stories, many layers of Is-Glue.

         

        Someone else having a story about something you care about is a scary proposition. To get away from the fear you squeeze out a bit of Is-Glue and create a story about their story. Unfortunately, this leaves you spending your time wading through this sticky Is-Glue story soup rather than being in touch with Reality herself.

         

        Say you watch one of the Possibility Films, you friend might come out saying "That was terrible". It was one of your favourites so you quickly create the story "They don't know anything about film. I'm really the connoisseur here!"

         

        This experiment is for the next three days to notice the stories you have about other people's stories, and even better the stories you have about their stories about your stories.

         

        At least once during each of these days, write in your Beep! Book about how things went, what you noticed about people and YOUR Is-Glued stories about THEIR Is-Glued stories.

         

        Then, enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.06 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

        DISCOVER THE LAYERS OF A STORY

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.08

        Stories have layers of story and purpose.

         

        For this experiment, pick someone you kind of get along with.

         

        In the middle of a conversation, notice something they have a story about, and ask "Can I ask you a question about this?"

         

        Then, when they say yes, ask the question, and when they answer, say, "why did you give me that answer?"

         

        Write down the answer in your Beep! Book.

         

        And then say, "Thank you!"

         

        And then say,

         

        "Why did you give me THAT answer?"

        or "Why is it important to you to have said THAT?"

        or "What is in it for you that you said THAT to me?"

         

        Take it down to each next level.

         

        Write each answer down, five layers down.

         

        Do this with three different people.

         

        Then you can enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.08 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

        THE "HOW ARE YOU" STORY

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.09

        "How are you?" and it's customary answers of "I'm good, thanks" are Box mantras designed to keep the conversation in the safe marshmallow zone of your Box's comfort zone.

         

        Each time you answer that question you create another Bullshit story about what's going on. It happens so often during the day that this is the perfect place to experiment.

         

        A great way to learn to use Is-Glue is to play with it in as many different ways as you can. This experiment is about playing with it in another way.

         

        For the whole week, every day for 7 days, when people ask you, "How are you?" give them five different answers.

         

        In each answer, use a different Is-Glue in every sentence.

         

        For example, start with "I am..." and then "I have..." etcetera.

         

        If they want you to stop, declare: "You asked me how I am, and I will tell you!"

         

        At the end of the week, write in your Beep! Book for at least 20 minutes about how things went, and then enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.09 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

        DISCOVER THE ABSURDITY OF STORIES

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.11

        This experiment is to approach absurdity with a consideration that stories are true. That you can speak without saying bullshit.

         

        Do this experiment when somebody asks you a question, or, if nobody asks you a question, then ask somebody to ask you a question.

         

        Then, in response, Is-Glue together a fake story without laughing or smiling.

         

        In other words, tell a story that you think is not true.

         

        Then stop and notice what goes on for you when you are aware of the fact that you are telling a story you think is not true.

         

        Next: tell the story that you lied, and why you lied.

         

        But lie about why you lied.

         

        Then lie about why you lied about lying.

         

        Then ask the person, "What's true about what I just said?"

         

        They will tell you a story.

         

        Ask them, "What's in it for you for giving me that answer?"

         

        Once you have done this, write about what happened and what you noticed in your Beep! Book for at least 30 minutes.

        LEARN TO USE YOUR PURPOSE SNIFFER

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.20

        You were born with a Purpose Sniffer as one of the 13 Tools on your Possibilitator's Toolbelt.

         

        Use it to sniff out the purpose behind the stories, behaviours, movements etc of yourself and those around you.

         

        The purpose could come from your Upperworld i.e. being in service to Bright Principles like Possibility, Love or Clarity. Or the purpose could come from the depths of your Underworld and serve Shadow Principles like Resentment, Revenge or Greed.

         

        Every story has a purpose behind it. This experiment is about sniffing it out.

         

        For the next three days, notice the stories other people tell you.

         

        For example, they might say: "I feel great today," or a story about their mom, about work, about the price of spaghetti...

        Say, "I noticed that of all the possible stories you could have told me, you told me <repeat the story they told you>."

         

        For example: "I feel great today," or the story about their mom, about work, about the price of spaghetti...

         

        "For my research, tell me the purpose, benefit and payoff behind you choosing to tell me that story."

         

        Write them down and say thank you.

         

        After three days, write in your Beep! Book for at least 20 minutes about how things went.

        DO THE "I HAVE A STORY ABOUT YOU" PROCESS

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.21

        Within seconds of meeting someone you will already have made many stories about that person.

         

        Which part of you is making those stories? If the purpose is Unconscious then it will be Your Box crafting stories to defend you or Your Gremlin crafting them to hurt the other person.

         

        It's time to find out where your stories are coming from.

         

        The "I Have a Story About You" process is one of dozens of dozens of processes on the PM Processes website. It is listed under the letter "I".

         

        1. Say "I have a story about you. My story is: I feel (angry|sad|scared|glad) because you........."
        2. Say "My evidence for my story is......"
        3. Say "My fear behind my story is......"
        4. Say "My payoff/Shadow Benefit for my story is......"
        5. Then ask the other person "What do you think and feel about my story?"

        Do it with one person.

         

        Then do it again in a Possibility Team or with your business team or knitting circle. Present the process. Send people into pairs to do the process together, including you.

         

        Share together about what it was like to do this.

         

        Then please enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.21 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.

         

        This Experiment, when you have done both parts, is worth 2 Matrix Points.

        CHANGE YOUR MOTHER WITH STORIES

        Matrix Code ISGLUExx.23

        The stories we tell create results in our lives, in our world.

         

        Take your Beep! Book and write down ten stories about your mother.

         

        Pick one.

         

        Consciously make a completely new story about your mother in regards to the first of the ten things. Write it down.

         

        For a week to ten days, tell the new story about your mother to the people around you, to your partner, to your friends.

         

        Then visit or call your mother and notice what happens.

         

        After some time noticing, without explaining, tell her the new story.

         

        Over the next few weeks or months, do it again with each of the other nine stories.

         

        Then please enter Matrix Code ISGLUExx.23 in your free account at StartOver.xyz.
