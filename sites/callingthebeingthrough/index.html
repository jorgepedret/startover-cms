  • Report from Ewa Szczepaniak

    Dear All,

    Last Sunday, in heptagonal living room at Ontree House, the village was calling my Being Through. This (R)evolutionary Villaging (https://www.ontreecentre.org/services) process was legendary as it was a first process held by Ontree (https://www.ontreecentre.org) at Ontree land.

    During the process, the energy of love, presence, and commitment to my Being was so big that my Box couldn’t resist it. I entered a place of surrendering, not knowing what is happening, and letting the forces go through me. The more I let go, the more 'weird' sensations, sounds, expressions happened in my bodies. I felt a lot of fear, and at the same time, so much trust to the village that it can hold me. There were moments in the process where I felt immense amount of energy running through my body. In the chakra integration part I felt that with this energy I am able to take all these people around me on my hands and lift them up. I never before felt so big and powerful.

    This process has a start, and it ends when it is finished. Being witnessed by the village and having no time pressure allowed me to surrender to it fully that brought another healing layer into the process. I felt loved and welcomed. I didn’t 'have to' do anything, I was feeling totally in peace occupying the space, being in silence for a while and just experiencing being together. It was a magical moment of deep connection.

    I feel so lucky to live in the village of people whose Love is so present in the space. I feel touched and glad writing this because I feel at Home here with people in the NZ Village. I love you.

    Ewa

    An article about the Initiation by Lisa Ommert

    [...] You do live in a world, where parents look forward to welcoming a physically healthy baby. (“What does she look like? Is she robust?”) Your parents are also most likely looking forward to having a bright child. (“How smart is she?”) In short, your mental and physical bodies are very appreciated. But, pretty sure, no one welcomed your three other bodies: the emotional (the emotions and feelings), the energetic (the soul, your Being) and the archetypal body (the life forces).

    The result is that half of your resources are disregarded, even banished. More than half of your presence and sensations are blocked from coming through and being here.

    In contrast, there are rumors that certain African tribes - the Himba - communicate with the Being of a baby even before the mother becomes pregnant. When the mother decides she is ready to receive a baby, the villagers come together and ask the Being what its purpose for being born is, how it will serve the village and what the Being will require in terms of education or support in order to best deliver its services. They even ask the Being to teach the villagers its soul-song so that when the baby is being born they can sing this song to welcome the Being along with the body, mind and heart.

    If a Being could have come through all by itself, it would have already done so. But since it has not, what this implies is that conditions that provide the Being with enough respect, honor, recognition, play space, love, and creative companionship so it would be attracted to come into the material plane did not yet exist.
