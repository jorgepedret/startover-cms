  • Poor Form

    Like many aspects of communication, Dragon Speaking succeeds through using 'proper form'. Sometimes the best way to learn 'proper form' is to study 'poor form'. Below we show you several examples of 'poor forms' of Dragon Speaking.

    Contrary to what you might think, using a microphone or megaphone blocks your Dragon. You are hiding behind the technology. Learn to do Dragon Speaking with your Real Voice. Yes... you may feel like you are shouting so that people can hear you. But hey... this is Dragon Speaking.

    Much of your Dragon Speaking communication comes through your eyes, not only your voice. Keep your eyes open and look directly into people's eyes when Dragon Speaking. Let them see you as well as hear you. And use your hands. Let your hands come back to life.

    This woman is hurting herself by pulling on her own hair. This turns the energy of Dragon Speaking back onto yourself. It breaks the first rule: Don't hurt yourself. By shouting at people you are not hurting their feelings. Yes, some people will love you, and some people will be afraid of you. This is how it always is. Respect people enough to let them have their own feelings.

    Dragon Speaking uses the pure conscious feeling of anger as the carrier energy for your communication. This woman is mixing another feeling in with her anger. Which feeling is she mixing in? Yes. She is mixing in fear. This mixture comes out as hysteria... very loud, but powerless. The power of Dragon Speaking comes from the Stand you take backed by your pure conscious adult anger.

    Dragon Speaking is about landing powerful transformational distinctions in the space you are holding. This woman is making accusations. She is blaming, shaming, resenting, threatening, hating... using the Parent Egostate to feed her Gremlin by assuming she is right and you are wrong. She is speaking against the other person instead of speaking for what she wants to say. Dragon Speaking is about saying what you want and don't want, about guarding what you love.
