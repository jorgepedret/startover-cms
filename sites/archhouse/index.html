        A NEW HUMAN CHALLENGE

        Humanity has arrived at an evolutionary stage where the necessity of learning new ways to come together and collaborate can no longer be ignored. The challenges that lie at our doorstep are considerable, and demand radical personal change.

         

        Almost every intelligent person dreams of more connection and community in their lives. Yet most did not learn the necessary soft skills growing up for how to harmoniously and effectively share space or create and sustain Gaian Gameworlds collaboratively.

         

        This is part of what we will be devoting and honing experientially in the living laboratory of the Arch House, along with the Possibilitator context and core skills.

         

        Every aspect of modern life as we have come to know it is unregenerative (read that as 'ecocidal', meaning, a death sentence for life on planet Earth).

         

        The known world is in a state of radical flux. It is groundless, and baseless, because modern culture is exterminating life on Earth at the fastest possible rate.

         

        All known procedures are in question... commerce, education, health, government, military, religion, economics, food production, lifestyle, transportation, clothing... but what are the answers? They too are unknown.

         

        This means that Arch House is, by necessity, a research center, sharing the findings from our experiments. In short, we have a lot of writing to do.

        SPACEHOLDER TRAINING

        In addition to practicalities such as Whole Permaculture, Torus Technology, Possibility Management, Gaian Gameworld-building, and 3 Phase Healing, your main takeaway will be Bridge-House Spaceholder Training.

         

        There is a massive necessity and hunger for Transformation. What we need now are spaceholders.

        There is a massive necessity and hunger for living in Regenerative Culture. What we need now are Gaian Gameworld Builders.

         

        The main purpose of the Arch Bridge-House is the train Spaceholders, train Spaceholder Trainers and train Gaian Gameworld Builders. Along these line, we are calling for Spaceholder of Specialty.

         

        The emergence of Archiarchy (Next Culture) is renewing, transforming, and discovering every facet of what makes a regenerative culture a regenerative culture from Whole Permaculture, to Children's Culture, to Women's Culture, to Men's Culture, to Magic, to Music, to Transformational Theater, to 5-Body doctors, to Communication with Animals, to Weaving a Village, to Decision Making, to Building Circles, etc...

        Each of the facets of Archiarchy are waiting to be inhabited, and therefore continuously evolved, from the Point-of-Origin of Radical Responsibility. Who will do it if not you? What is your Specialty? What facet of Archiarchy Next Culture is it your job (read your ecstasy) to hold space for?

         

        When you enter the game and play at the level of taking Radical Responsibility for your Specialty, you meet other people who are playing the same game. Welcome to Arch House & Team!

        What we learn is that no one but you (and the other people in your Specialty) can hold space for your Specialty.

         

        The good news is that basic, foundational, core Spaceholder Skills (also called Possibilitator Skills) and Context are transferable from any Specialty to another. This is the focus of Arch House & Team.

        YOUR SPECIALTY

        You do not get to choose your Specialty. Your Specialty chooses you.

         

        How can you tell when you Specialty has choosen you? When you wake up in the morning, and there is only one thing you want to do: Your Specialty, that is a good sign that your Specialty has found you.

         

        How do you get from where you are right now to having your Specialty find you? You ernestly and enthusiastically try Creation experiments. Creation experiments are very different from criticizing, reacting, tearing down, being overwhelmed, blaming your imaginary 'authority figure', ...

         

        Ernestly doing Creation experiments means that when you make an agreement, you complete it or you communicate. Ernestly doing Creation experiments means you experiment even if you do Not Knowing How To. A Creation experiment reflexively puts you in a Liquid State. If you avoid the Liquid State, you avoid Creation. Ernestly means you are awareness and willing to go through the Liquid State as you Create. Ernestly doing Creation experiments becomes an ecstatic possibility when you have stabilize yourself into your Adult Egostate, hence the requirement for you to enter the Possibilitator Training. Without a strong foot in your Adult Egostate, your ernestness is too easily capture by your Child Egostate wanting to complete the task for the teacher, or your Parent Egostate manipulating you and beating you up if you do not complete the task. This is not ernestness, both of those are torture.

        Archiarchy is not a survival culture. Please be well on your way to heal from your familiar survial mechanisms before applying to the Arch House & Team.

        Enthusiastically doing Creation experiments is far away from living in a pink bubble of fantasy world that you will feel nothing but Joy while doing Creation experiments with the Arch Team. Yes, there will be Joy, massive amounts of it. As much as you will feel deep Rage, immense Fear and profound Sadness. Enthuastically doing Creation experiments means it turns you on to:

        • Try things you have never tried before
        • Empower and engage a Team to support you and support each other in their own experiment
        • Create Possibility for each other along the way
        • Gifting yourself and the Team with the intelligence and resources of your Infinite Inner and Outer Resources
        The more you engage in different Creation experiments, the clearer your Specialty will design itself for you.

        It became obvious then that their next Creation Experiment was to build a Team of Edgeworkers, looking for Creation experiments, to work together to make those Creation happen. The offer and the demand can meet!

        The conversation for the Arch House & Team is not: "Oh! We could do this too! Or what about this? Here's another Possibility!"

        Theoretical Possibilities is not what we are lacking. What we are missing are Edgeworkers with their own Agency and Authority to make these Possibilities occur in Reality.

        WRITE 'THREE THINGS I WANT TO CHANGE'

        Matrix Code ARCHHOUS.16

        Conscious pain is about the only motivation strong enough to drive someone to make a lifestyle change. New awarenesses that make you angry enough to try something new come from lowering your marshmallow-sweet buffer zone, letting the delusion dissolve, and finding out what is really going on.

         

        For this Experiment, please read through the What Now website... slowly.

         

        While reading the words and watching the videos, lower your Numbness Bar so that your mind does not skip over the painful realizations.

         

        Then write a 2 to 3 page article titled: Three Things I Want To Change. Let your nonlinear intelligence choose the three things you are angry enough about to move into a life that leaves these three things out. In your article, explain practical alternative possibilities that you would prefer to have in your life to replace the three things from which you will step away.

         

        This, obviously, is a personal article, meaning that there are no objectively true answers about what you would prefer. Which is super! Because it means you cannot get it wrong! Please publish your article online at http://medium.com.

         

        After you have published your article, please register Matrix Code ARCHHOUS.01 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. Please give the link to your article as the PROOF. This Experiment is worth 2 Matrix Points.

        CAVITATE NEW CULTURE SPACE THAT YOU WOULD LOVE TO LIVE IN

        Matrix Code ARCHHOUS.17

        It can be a shock to realize that next culture - Archiarchy - is NOT already built and ready for you to occupy like the culture of the Na'vi was for Jake Sully to move into in James Cameron's film Avatar.

         

        There is nearly nothing ready for you. If you want a different culture, you will need to design it, build it, and move into by your own volition.

         

        You probably do not have the balls, the determination, the fierceness to do that. Instead you will probably forget that you read this Experiment and simply go on to something less painful.

         

        But hey! You don't have to. You could take me seriously. (I take myself seriously...) This Experiment is to skip over the comforting sense of, "I don't know," then enter the present (where you cannot already know because in the present all you get is improvisation and creation), and choose 3 specific qualities or elements of a culture you would love to live in.

         

        Grab your Beep! Book and write the three elements out, like a free-form poem. Do not try to make it nice. (Every new idea at first sounds crazy!)

         

        Then for each of your 3 qualities, write out 3 specific actions you would take if that quality was the culture you truly lived in.

         

        Now you have 9 Experiments to try (3 actions for each of the 3 qualities). During this next week, do each of these 9 behaviors three times. Do each one with dynamic fervor, ferocious commitment, indubitable enthusiasm, and joyful thoroughness, so your behavior lands totally in the current space in everyone's awareness and everyone's 5 Bodies, but do the behavior for YOUR OWN ENJOYMENT, not to do anything to the other people. Do not try to change anyone else. They are living in the culture they want to live in. So you get to live in the culture you want to live in! It is only fair, right? Have a hell of a good time, right there in front of the others without any excuse.

         

        After you have accomplished all 27 of these actions, please register Matrix Code ARCHHOUS.00 in your free account at StartOver.xyz. In the PROOF section write 12 words about what you learned. This Experiment is worth 3 Matrix Points.
