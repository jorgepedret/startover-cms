        YOUR POTENTIAL DETECTOR

        You have a built in Potential Detector, somewhere near your Solar Plexus. It is definitely NOT in your mind, and it is very different from the Feelings Detector in your Heart.

         

        It is not your Bullshit Detector.

         

        It is not your Gremlin Detector, although your Potential Detector can sense Gremlin offers before they are made.

         

        Your Potential Detector has a particular sensitivity to which words, movements, offers, withholds, etc. move towards the Ordinary World, and which words, silences, gestures, movements, offers, withholds etc. move towards the Extraordinary.

         

        Detecting Potential feels like sensing when a Distinction lands in a Space or not, or how the Distinction is landing in a person's Being.

         

        A distinction is an energetic slice through an experience. When a distinction lands in the space, the space moves: it might changes shape, direction, quality or you might even move to a whole new space in the great labyrinth of spaces. The possibilities available in the space are different before and after the distinction. What you can increase is your Potential Sensor, your sensitivity to potential: how is the quality of the space, and the possibility available in the space changed by the offers/the distinctions (or lack of) made in the space.

         

        For example, most people - accurately their Boxes - will make offer move away from the edges of the space/their Box in the marshmallow zone where they know they can survive: by disagreeing, or agreeing, by trying to be right, by speaking general platitude and theory, by being nice or throwing oil on the fire, by using Channel 1 or Channel 2 of Gremlin, etc...
        In this case, they are using ordinary move to keep the space ordinary.

         

        The Potential Sensor sense / smells / sees / experiences both moves made towards less activated potential and moves made towards more activated potential. The potential can be of one individual, a team, or a whole space.

         

        An addition to your experiment would be to not only sense the potential that you liberate - and I am adding that you inhibits - but also the potential that other people liberate or inhibits.

         

        For example, in this message: almost every sentence is a distinction. And each distinction moves along the way to liberate more potential through awareness of something that we were not aware of before, including me! Can you sense how when you read through each sentence the space available to you moves?

         

        That is your Potential Sensor!

        This is FUN! (and that is not a distinction, it is just an experience!)
