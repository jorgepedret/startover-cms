  • Millicent Haughey

    It is rare to find a woman who holds space like a wizard.

    Flying through the vast emptiness enabling the next real thing, Anne-Chloé sources the impossible time after time. Essential bolts of fear are unleashed, running like lightning through the space, cracking crusted layers of survival strategies, masks and bullshit.

    Anne-Chloe's dedication to each edgeworker’s authentic, radiant, brilliant Being means she does exactly what it takes to hold a space safe, alive, enticing and real enough for Genius to step forward upright, dignified, heart-beating and oh so alive.

  • Clinton Callahan

    Author of Cavitation: the emergence of Archiarchy.
    Originator of Possibility Management.
    Co-creator of the StartOver.xyz game.

    If you imagine someone who is certified as a lawyer and an economist with the discipline and focus of a world-class athlete, closely examining the memetic structure of your inner world and your project design, with the intention of birthing you into regenerative human culture and radical relating 5-body intimacy skills, while applying a wizard's nonlinearity, a sorceress's fierceness, a goddess's love, and a possibilitator's humor, then you comprehend the transformational effectiveness and thrill of participating in whatever is offered by Anne-Chloé Destremau.

  • Christine Dürschner

    Anne-Chloé embodies clarity, love and possibility from head to toe. She sources inspiration through accompanying our experimental journey and creating awesome from the unknown in each present moment.

    Her ability to see ways to transform individuals also as a larger collective - such as a village or community - is extraordinary. Her ability to transform obstacles into doorways with her wildness and her open heart is thrillingly effective. Her way of facing complexity and distilling it into clear distinctions opens possibilities that were previously out of reach.

    I am truly grateful to be on the path with this amazing woman. Thank you for holding me and us accountable for our own ability to create.

  • Christina Beberdick

    During a messy and wonderfully ecstatic, yet painful process, you were by my side looking straight into my eyes and connecting with me through profoundly authentic rage and commitment. I was so deeply supported by you while immersed in my own evolution. You would not let me slip when I wanted to give up or deceive myself.

    You love and trust in the magic of transformation. I found the strength to let go of control and make it to the 'other side' - tired and sweaty, shaking with aliveness and Joy.

    In Anne-Chloé's space, I experience both having a Team and being on a Team.

  • Isabel Esterl

    Thank you for being on my team so I could build enough inner structure to be the Woman who I am right now. I am so glad you did see what was off.

    Every day now, I am more committed to life and having more choices then I ever had before.

    I love you. You did not give up on me. You held space for me in the first six months of my process and your presence made so much difference.

    My edge is now to relate authentically and to let the distinctions integrate in my Being.

  • Jacqueline O'Neill

    In the hours leading up to Introduction to Conscious Fear, I watched a number of the STARRS you delivered and I felt you with me. I was incredibly empowered by the clarity and love with which you deliver your distinctions.

    Thank you for your devotion to your services and your transformational generosity.
    I am touched and inspired by you.

    Your impact on Earth is real, loving, and beautiful.
