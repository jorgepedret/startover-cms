  • FLYING INSTRUCTIONS FOR INDIVIDUALS

    These are the seven instructions for flying. These are all the flying instructions you will ever need. If you can live in them, please try to teach them to others. There is plenty of empty space out here to fly in...Have fun! And, thanks for asking.(Based on the book Building Love That Lasts by Clinton Callahan, previously titled Radiant Joy Brilliant Love.)

    1

    Do not ask “How do I fly?”

    To fly, do not ask the question, “How do I fly?”

    Asking this question is a distraction from flying. Asking the question, “How do I fly?” is a defense against flying.

    Your 5 Bodies already have the capacity for flight.

    Human beings are designed to Hold Spaces in which humans can fly.

    Initiated adult Human beings are not intended to live within the positional confines of a rigidly known psychological defense strategy.

    You are designed to live in the fluidic groundless unknowable process of evolutionary expansion.

    Flying is your nature.

    From here on, your Path is Practice.

    2

    Groundcheck.

    Before takeoff it can be crucially important to make a Groundcheck.
    'Groundcheck' is what a pilot does for an airplane before taking off into the air. She walks around the plane to make sure the shields are taken off the jet intakes, that no birds built nests in between the cylinders of the engine in the hinge gaps of the vertical stabilizer, that the chocks are removed from the wheels, that no bees laid their eggs in and sealed over the pitot tube blocking the air speed indicator, that there is enough air pressure in the tires, that the gyroscope comes up to speed for the compass to work, that the hydraulic systems are filled with hydraulic fluid and not leaking pressure, that the water condensation is drained out of the bottom of the gas tank so the engine does not stall, that the radio frequencies work, that her flight plan has been coordinated with the Air Traffic Controller, all this and more before even starting the engines.
    There is an equivalent Groundcheck to do before takeoff in the Spaces you Navigate. It can include making sure the kids are well handled, that there is food in the pantry, that the bills are paid, the lights are turned off, the fires banked. It can include telling people that you have changed your mind about something imporant so that you may behave differently than they are accustomed to expecting from you, that you will be doing an Experiment which may look like very weird behavior to them but that you will be okay. It can include simply telling people where you might be going, and that you will be back probably around a certain time. It can include making sure you have your passport and your pocket knife and enough cash in the coin of the realm in your pockets. And will certainly include making sure you have your Center, Grounding Cord, Bubble, 13 Tools, Gremlin at your side, Possibility Paintbrush to hand, and you Beep! Book along and a pen with enough ink in the cartridge to document the diagrams and notes from the Treasures you discover while Flying.

    3

    Do not look down.

    As soon as you look down, the Box gets linear and sees all the well-known “problems” inherent to flying.

    “Hmmm, I wonder how high we are? What’s the air speed and temperature up here? How much fuel do we have left? How did we get up this high anyway? What is that thing down there? AAAARRRGGGHHH!”

    Crash!

    Practice nonlinearity. This skill helps you shift from butting heads with well-defended realities, to choosing from possible options that are mostly invisible to others until you implement them before their eyes and have a great time of it.

    4

    Take yourself lightly.

    The idea is old and sounds trite, but that does not make it untrue: Love is 'angel food'. Angels can fly because they take themselves lightly. Stay light by slowly spinning your Energetic Body (clockwise when you look down) constantly dropping the baggage of considerations about past and future. Humor is grease to slip free of seriousness.

    NOW is nearly weightless. If it is heavy it is not Love. Stay Unhookable by staying connected to the Nothingness both inside of you and outside of you. Nothingness cannot be Hooked by anything. A small Disk Of Nothing helps to keep you Angel-weight.

    5

    Delight.

    Yes. 'Delight' is a complete instruction with regards to flying. Delight is also a verb.

    Answer these questions:

    What delights you? (You. Not your Box. Not your Gremlin. Not your mother or father or society. You. This is an important Question to keep answering for yourself, by yourself.)

    What delights the other? (Only they can answer this question. Your job is to keep asking that question, and truly Listen.)

    Use those two questions as your primary compass.

    Play.

    Explore.

    Go Unreasonable.

    Totally stop.

    Be.

    Open.

    Try again.

    Stay at the Edge.

    G[r>jak#L*!

    (This is the spelling for a delightful activity for which we do not yet have a word. There are many such un-labeled delightful experiences. Try to find them all.)

    Navigate the space of relationship towards delight happening.

    Why not?

    Remember, Authentic delight is alive, so you will always have a moving target.

    What is delightful now is not necessarily delightful now. Delight only happens now.

    6

    Create.

    Relationship is ongoing nonlinear creation. Love and creating are the same experience; they come out of the same field.

    You are a Being who lives when Love flows through you, when you arrange for someone to give Love to and they receive it, even if they do not know you.

    Keep inventing new ways to express Love.

    Create ways to love out of nothing but Love itself. You source Love.

    Love does not come from somebody else.

    Love is all there is.

    Instead of using your massive powers of creating to create things NOW to be exactly how they were three seconds ago, use your massive powers of creating to create Love fresh each three seconds.

    7

    Learn to fly solo.

    You will never be able to be happy with another person if you cannot already tolerate the intensity of being radiantly happy all by yourself. No matter what the circumstances, if you really wanted to be happy, who could stop you?

    If you really want to be happy right now, prove it.

    8

    So what?

    There are many reasons for not flying. So what? Many people will not understand what you are doing. They might be offended. They might not believe you. So what?

    Flying might be impossible. It might not be permitted by your culture and times. It might be outside the conceivability limits of your relatives or neighbors. So what?

    You get the idea.

    So what?

  • THE INVITATION by Oriah Mountain Dreamer

    It doesn't interest me
    what you do for a living.
    I want to know
    what you ache for
    and if you dare to dream
    of meeting your heart's longing.

     

    It doesn't interest me
    how old you are.
    I want to know
    if you will risk
    looking like a fool
    for love
    for your dream
    for the adventure of being alive.

     

    It doesn't interest me
    what planets are
    squaring your moon...
    I want to know
    if you have touched
    the center of your own sorrow
    if you have been opened
    by life's betrayals
    or have become shriveled and closed
    from fear of further pain.

     

    I want to know
    if you can sit with pain
    mine or your own
    without moving to hide it
    or fade it
    or fix it.

     

    I want to know
    if you can be with joy
    mine or your own
    if you can dance with wildness
    and let the ecstasy fill you
    to the tips of your fingers and toes
    without cautioning us
    to be careful
    to be realistic
    to remember the limitations
    of being human.

     

    It doesn't interest me
    if the story you are telling me
    is true.

    I want to know if you can
    disappoint another
    to be true to yourself.
    If you can bear
    the accusation of betrayal
    and not betray your own soul.
    If you can be faithless
    and therefore trustworthy.

     

    I want to know if you can see Beauty
    even when it is not pretty
    every day.
    And if you can source your own life
    from its presence.

     

    I want to know
    if you can live with failure
    yours and mine
    and still stand at the edge of the lake
    and shout to the silver of the full moon,

    "Yes."

     

    It doesn't interest me
    to know where you live
    or how much money you have.

     

    I want to know if you can get up
    after the night of grief and despair
    weary and bruised to the bone
    and do what needs to be done
    to feed the children.

     

    It doesn't interest me
    who you know
    or how you came to be here.
    I want to know if you will stand
    in the center of the fire
    with me
    and not shrink back.

     

    It doesn't interest me
    where or what or with whom
    you have studied.
    I want to know
    what sustains you
    from the inside
    when all else falls away.

     

    I want to know
    if you can be alone
    with yourself
    and if you truly like
    the company you keep
    in the empty moments.
