        • Thoughtware Upgrade For Yourself

          CONSEQUENCES

          Albert Einstein famously said, "We cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them." What he neglected to explain is how to change your thinking.

           

          Your Thoughtware is what you use to think with. At school they teach you what to think about, ignoring what you use to think with. The school curriculum assumes (erroneously...) that each child already learned to think the same way from their parents.

           

          You built your original Thoughtware out of 'memes'. Similar in function to genes, memes are the smallest instruction for the design of your intellectual body... your mind. Memes can be used to build any kind of Thoughtware. Yes. And. The Thoughtware you use determines how the world works for you. The design of your Thoughtware has consequences.

           

          If you inspect the outcome of modern culture, so far, it mirrors modern culture's Standard Human Intelligence Thoughtware.

           

          As a child you assemble your thoughtware out of memes supplied by your environment because this empowers you to survive in those conditions. Later on, after you survived to eighteen years old, your meme-set - what Possibility Management refers to as your Box - is ready to be escaped, like a chick at the proper time escapes its shell.

           

          But if you step outside of your Box, who are you?

          EDGEWORK

          Upgraded Thoughtware is available, but your Thoughtware will not be upgraded at school, because modern culture avoids explaining the possibility of shifting context to create new thoughtware. Such an explanation can only be given at the edge of the culture, where you first get a view of the entire culture from a neutral perspective.

           

          Your birth culture is just a Gameworld, one of many Gameworlds operating simultaneously in the global human ethnosphere on Earth. Perhaps you still remember the moment you got to the edge of modern culture’s worldview. (You would probably not be reading this unless you already found that edge…)

           

          Your first thought at the edge might be, “Oh! It has an edge!” Suddenly the Gameworld of modern culture becomes visible as a whole. The capitalistic patriarchal empire spreads out behind you with so many people claiming it is ‘true’, believing that it is the ‘best thing human beings ever invented’. Whatever they believe, there is now evidence to suggest this is not the case. As Ishmael author Daniel Quinn observes, “Something better than civilization is waiting for us.”

           

          Your second thought at the edge might be, “Ah! Something completely different from this is possible right now!”

           

          Then a third thought comes, “Holy Criminy! I feel scared!” And if you use modern culture’s Standard Human Intelligence Thoughtware about fear, you think fear is one of those ‘negative feelings’, a ‘bad feeling’, something to be avoided at all costs or people will lock you away and give you drugs. The fear makes your whole system contract, bouncing reflexively back into the marshmallow zone at the Center of your Box. Every Edgeworker knows that they may encounter thoughtware-upgrade Doorways ten times a day. Unconscious fears would automatically bounce them back into their thought-prisons of their Box each time. Instead, they make their fears Conscious and say, “Welcome fear! What do you have for me?” And then they let the intelligence and energy of their Conscious Fear inform them with its gifts of clarity. They complete the communication from their fears, and use this resource as rocket fuel for intelligently inventing and inhabiting the next new part of archearchy.

           

          Upgrading to a New Thoughtmap of Feelings changes everything because it provides the clarity for reconnecting to exactly the inner and outer resources needed for becoming adult. On the New Thoughtmap of Feelings, fear is no longer ‘negative’ or ‘bad’. Instead fear is powerful neutral energy and incredibly precise information about what to take care of, what to watch out for, how to stay alert and attentive, how to take care of yourself around people who are untrustworthy so that you can actually trust yourself to take care of yourself around people like them, how to pay attention and navigate the void of the unknown and still function to create what is wanted and needed by your village now while standing on nothing because it was never invented before. Does this sound useful? You get there through initiation.

Most of how it goes to upgrade thoughtware for individuals is doing the healing, initiation, and transformation processes that prepare them to jack-in to their particular archetypal lineage.           But there is a serious shift that needs to occur before the archetypal lineage jacking-in process can take place.

           

          One of the most powerful ways to describe this profound shift has to do with the way a Possibilitator (Thoughtware Upgrader) delivers the distinctions in a thoughtmap drawn on a flipchart board.

           

          Before the context shift, the Possibilitator stands in front of the Client (or the group) and imagines themselves imparting new knowledge to the Client following the only role model they have for 'imparting knowledge to another': the 'school 'teacher'. They clear their throat and say, "I have some valuable knowledge which I think I understand in my mind that I think you should know, so I will explain these ideas to you. Then after I tell you, you will understand these new ideas in your mind." This 'teacher' draws the Thoughtmap on flip-chart paper according to their own memory of how it looks (or copies a drawing by looking at notes or cards), and hopes that at the end, the 'student' understands.

           

          After the context shift, the Possibilitator approaches Thoughtware Upgrade entirely differently. They walk into the Training Space focusing on their own Center, Grounding Cord, and Bubble, greets the Presiding Deity Of The Space, then Declares the 8 points of their Energetic Work Space to include the Client (or the whole Team) online-or-offline. The Possibilitator calls their Bright Principles into that Space, checks with E.C.C.O. about any coincidences, and speaks as a Space through which their Bright Principles and Archetypal Lineage can do their work. The Possibilitator's speaking carries extra value from the Sword Of Clarity in their hand, and their Initiated Gremlin alert at their side. When it would be useful to deliver a Thoughtmap, the Possibilitator knows nothing. Instead the Possibilitator stays in experiential connection with each Co-Journeyer with one part of their Split Attention, and with another part of their Split Attention the Possibilitator moves their Point Of Origin to the birth-place of the Thoughtmap - the actual pristine Space out of which the Distinctions and energetic diagrams of this particular Thoughtmap originally emerged. That Space still exists. With one 'foot' standing in the Space of Clarity where the Thoughtmap was born, and their other 'foot' connecting to the Matrix of each Client in the Team, the Possibilitator uses Memetic Speaking to allow the Thoughtmap to build itself inside of the Client. The Possibilitator is not explaining the ideas of the Thoughtmap to the Client. Instead the Possibilitator weaves the Thoughtmap inside of the Client until the Client becomes the Thoughtmap. The Client does not have to try to understand or try to remember the Thoughtmap because they have become the Thoughtmap. They are now someone new. Their shape has changed to such a degree that the Universe has no option but to interact with them differently.

           

          This evolved procedure for landing Thoughtmaps in the Client is but one of thousands of skills and tools needed to serve as the Possibilitator Specialty developed in Phase 2 of Possibilitator Training called Possibility Trainer.
