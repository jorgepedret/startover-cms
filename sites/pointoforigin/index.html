  • Finding The Point Of Origin You Stand On Now

    YOUR POINT OF ORIGIN 

    Most human beings are rather completely unaware of their Point Of Origin.

     

    Remaining unaware of your Point Of Origin is an effective Survival Strategy.

     

    If you remain unaware of your Point Of Origin then you have no power to change it. This means you continue to live out of your current Point Of Origin, the one you received from your parents, your schooling, your culture, and your times.

     

    By avoiding taking Radical Responsibility for your current and potential Points Of Origin, you also avoid serving your Bright Principles and your Archetypal Lineage. You also remain rather uninteresting to the Earth Coincidence Control Office (E.C.C.O.).

     

    Remaining uninteresting to archetypal forces of nature is a personal choice.

     

    No on can relocate your Point Of Origin for you.

     

    No one can stop you from relocating your Point Of Origin.

    LOCATING YOUR POINT OF ORIGIN

    It is necessary to locate your Point Of Origin before you can relocate your Point Of Origin.

     

    Makes sense, doesn't it?

     

    How can you do whatever is necessary to decide if you want to relocate your Point Of Origin if you don't know where it already is and why you have kept it exactly there - perhaps unconsciously - for all this time?


    If you are not excruciatingly aware of the location and Purpose that your Point Of Origin is currently tied to, how can you even grab ahold of it?

  • Procedure For How To Relocate Your Point Of Origin

    1

    PREPARE

    First, study about what your Point Of Origin is, what it does for you, where it came from, and what else might be possible for you if your Point Of Origin were located somewhere else. Also see Thoughtware Upgrade #31 at the 50 Thoughtware Upgrades website. Before you even think of starting, make sure you are already skilled in Conscious Feelings, enough to have already done about 50 personal Emotional Healing Processes (EHP). It is not valuable to think you can do this Self Surgery and then fail becaue you were not actually prepared to do it. It sets you back. Don't do that to yourself.

    2

    PERFORM

    Start standing in First Position, Centered, Grounded, Bubbled, Sword Of Clarity to hand, Awed, and Improvising. Have a skilled Spaceholder holding Space for this Process. It is going to be loud, and you need a Witness, a second Consciousness. Be clear about the qualities and characteristics of the Context in which your Point Of Origin is currently planted. Then grab it by the roots. The Point Of Origin is an energetic anchor between your Being and a cultural Context. It looks like a gigantic brown carrot, a meter or so long, conical, buried deep into the muck. You need to pull it all the way out. All the way. All of it.

    Screaming, shouting, yelling, wrestling... it has been in there for decades, probably longer than that. It assumes it has not other options, that it was one with the muck. You are making the Distinction. "No! You are not the muck! You are distinct, my Point Of Origin. I have other plans for you. Now, come out of there!"

    Dragging, wrestling, tugging, screaming your commands. Get it out.

    This takes 5 minutes or so. If you are not struggling with all 5 of your Bodies to make this manuever, forget it. You will stay stuck in your head. You have to actually pull your Point Of Origin all the way out of your old context, breaking off all the tiny hair roots if necessary. You do it.

    When it is done, rest some moments.

    Check yourself out. How does it feel to be so groundless, to be so unrooted, to not have your Point Of Origin planted anywhere?

    Then the Spaceholder says, "Go stand on your new Context. What is it? What is it about? Tell me one Distinction from your new Context!"

    You shout it at her. "One Distinction of my new Context is:____!"

    Then you go!

    You dig and plant and dig and push, shouting the whole time, using the whole of the power of your 5 Bodies to make Space in the new Context for your Point Of Origin. Pound it in. Rage it in. Call it in. Wrestle it in there. Bury it deep into the new Context. Jumping on it to go all the way in. Until another 5 minutes has passed and finally it is in.

    Then you can feel the joy of it. The grief of having pretended to live in a false Context for so many years, being so adaptive, being so disempowered, being so far away from home.

    And finally, at last, you have planted your Point Of Origin in a place that suits you, that feeds you, that loves you being who you are.

    Celebrate your victory, together with your Spaceholder.

    3

    PROVE

    Sleep a lot. Rest. Do not watch TV or movies. Do not check your phone for a couple days. It is recouperation time for you.

    Drink lots of fresh water. You have been in a Self Surgery of major proportions. You do not actually know where you are now.

    After a day or two of resting, sleeping, walking in the trees or the sand or the snow, swimming in the ocean or the lake, watching the living Earth around you live in the ways that it lives, then do some small Experiments. Gently test out how it feels to be your unrestricted self. Feel. Be with a friend and feel your great sadness. There is a lot to grieve. Feel your fear. Ask, "Fear, what do you have for me?" and write down everything it says. Feel your anger at what is, your rage at what could be but is not. Feel your joy of aliveness. Do not rush too fast back into life. These are precious hours just after your Self Surgery. Enjoy their specialness, their tenderness.

    When you are ready to step out into the world again, go slowly. You still have brain grooves from your previous Context. But your impulses will be new. Let the new impulses win over your grooves. They will feel strange, your impulses to speak, where to put your attention and your energy. Let the strangeness lead you. You are rooted into new territory. Explore it gently. Make notes in your Beep! Book about what is strange and new and different. There is more before you now (you can see further and in more dimensions) and there is more behind you now (more resources, more support for your Potentials to come alive in the world). There is a lot new. Try to never leave the perception that it is all new for you now. Trust the new ground you stand on. Create the results you have been waiting for someone else to create for you. It is your time now. You are the one on fire! Make no important decisions for the next 3 days.

    WHAT IS THE NAME OF THIS PLACE / BY CAR

    Matrix Code POINTOFO.04

    Sit in your car and turn off the navigation system if you have one. Drive over smaller roads until you don't know where you are. (This can be a few hours or days and it is possible that the experiment will last several days).
    If you have no idea where you are at all, turn off your car and get out. What can you use to orient yourself?

    What does the position of the sun tell you about where you might be? When you see a person, ask them what the name of the place you are in is. Also ask about some details or special features of that place, for example:
    - What special sights are there in that place? (Then go there if you want, without having them explain how to get there).
    - What is the specialty that you must try here and where can I get it? (Then go to that place if you want, without having the way to get there explained to you).
    - What special people live in this place? (Visit them if you like).
    Make a decision about which direction to continue. Stop from time to time and ask someone what the name of this place is that you are in.
    The experiment is to never ask anyone how to get to any known place, but always ask where you are right now and continue from there.
    All the time, draw in your BEEP!Book the new points where you are and connect them. Also write the information that you hear from others or that you gather yourself and that seems important to you.

    BLIND

    Matrix Code POINTOFO.07

    Ask a person to help you carry out an experiment. Go to a pedestrian zone together. Blindfold your eyes so that you are blind. Then take a walk through the city together. Your companion supports you by making sure you don't get run over or run into a lamppost.

    Experiment with variations:
    - Your companion walks hand in hand with you and gives you information about the surroundings and the current destination: "We are now on the west side of the train station and walk towards the subway station." At the same time, your companion decides where the two of you will walk.
    - Your companion walks with you while being in physical contact with you. For example, she might put a hand on your shoulder or put a hand in your jacket pocket while you walk. You decide where to walk next, and the companion gives you information about the surroundings as you walk. "We're walking toward a shoe store right now. Do you want to go inside?" Make a decision and keep walking.
    - Your companion walks close beside you and gives you information about possible dangers. She touches you only in case of emergency and does not talk to you otherwise.
    Throughout the experiment, you can notice what possibilities you have with all 5 bodies and all senses to guide you. There are 4 feelings: anger, fear, sadness and joy.

    Which ones do you notice and why are they there now? ("I feel anger/fear/sadness/joy because...")
