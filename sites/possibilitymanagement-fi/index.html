      • Invitation

        Our offer is to bring Possibility Management with its Tools, Distinctions, Thoughtware, Meeting Technologies, Healing and Transformation processes to you, the English and Finnish-speaking Edgeworkers of Finland.

         

        Since 1998, Expand The Box training and Possibility Labs have been available in Europe... but not until 2021 have they been available in Finland.

         

        By updating what you use for thinking, and by learning the skills of intimate five-body navigation, you transform almost anything that gets in the way of living the life you want to live.

         

        If you are interested in this Possibility, please contact us!

         

        There is no end to managing possibilities, but there IS a beginning. The start for Finland is 2021.

The main complaint of participants is: Where is the community? Sadness and frustration prevail.

         

        EXTRAORDINARY STRATEGY: You meet with three people who are already deeply committed to a single Context. Together you deepen the Context. Start to design your Gaian Gameworld so that it feeds and nurtures Gaia.
