      • Women of Earth Fund

        The Women of Earth Fund is a self-organized micro-loan gameworld for the Women of Earth.

        Centered on Radical Responsibility, Empowerment and Love.

        The Women of Earth Fund works in the image of how the Women of Earth, in the cultural context of Archiarchy, have already been flowing Nonmaterial Value towards each other. The currencies are Emotional Healing Processes, Listening Spaces, our Sword of Clarity at the neck of our Gremlins, Grief Fearful and Raging spaces, Standing for each other and for our resonant mission. We have given birth to more Creative Collaboration that we can now count.

         

        It seems an appropriate time to take the next step in our empowerment of each other and flow financial resources among us. This is one more step to break the taboo of 'money' in a culture where money is far from central to our way of life. The Fund is equally a space for healing antiquated thoughtware about scarcity, whether it is financial or otherwise.

         

        ♾ The purpose of Flowing Funds is to empower a Woman at a very specific moment in time to 'get over the hump' of any financial necessity to become the thing that source Abundance through Creation. Sometimes, that 'hump' requires a mini-Flash Flood of Funds. I want those Funds to come from us instead of Patriarchal banks.

         

        ♾ The purpose of the Fund is to Flow money to you, Women of Earth, when you need it and for a specific need.

        There are two categories of necessity that you can apply for at this time:

        1. For a Nonmaterial Value Archan project.
        2. To empower your Evolutionary Path by flowing money towards Trainings.

         

        ♾ The Flow Proposals are made directly to the Fund, and then we - the Fund Team - check the viability of the proposal. We check:

        • Your Purpose: are you in Low Drama or High Drama about your proposal? We clearly look at any Victim/Rescuer dynamic between you and the Fund;
        • Any Hidden Competing Commitments such as: take Revenge, hold Resentments, maintain Beliefs that women will betray you, etc;
        • Your level of Accountability: can you make a promise and keep it?;
        • Your level of Relatibility: your ability to communicate in moments of stress, emotionality and fear;
        • Your Emotional stability: mostly in the demonstrating that you are going through EHPs that are showing up along your Path;
        • Your Team building ability: can you ask for a Team and stay in contact with that Team for your own Evolution?;
          We check these and more over a period of time.

        What is great about this is that the spaceholders: Anne-Chloé Destremau, Lisa Ommert, Vera Franco, Ana Norambuena have been with you for a while, so these things have been checked before you apply for the Flow Proposal, and how you are evolving in regards to them.

         

        ♾ A Flow Proposal is:

        1. A clear ask for a sum of money to Flow towards you within a certain period of time.

        2. A transparent Excel sheet where you write down the Flow received from each Woman.

        3. A clear stand about how you plan to FlowBack the money to the Flow Givers.

         

        ♾ The Women of Earth Fund works similarly to KIVA micro-loan. Meaning the Fund is not a bank. The Fund does not hold money in a bank account where the money stays. The money Flows among us directly from the Flow-Giver to the Flow-Receiver.

         

        ♾ The Flow Proposals are send out publicly by the Fund Team, and not the Flow-Receiver.

         

        ♾ Where do we all come in? Right now, most of us will be Flow-Givers. When you are inspired by a Flow Proposal, or receive the message that a Proposal needs your Flow, that you send the Flow to the Woman in question, plus let us know in the Telegram 'Women of Earth' group so you can be celebrated.

         

        ♾ When you are at a place to be a Flow Receiver, please reach out to Lisa, Vera or myself so we can give you your next steps on your Path, whether it is to make a Flow Proposals or take other action steps.

         

        ♾ For now, the Women of Earth Fund is only available for Women who have participated in a Women of Earth Lab with Ana, Anne-Chloé and Vera.

      • How The Fund Works

        The steps to creatively collaborate with the Women of Earth Fund.

        These steps are not in a particular order.

        Each Women need to build matrix is different areas at different moments.

        These steps cannot be 'done as fast as possible' so you can apply to the Fund. For most of you, by those steps, your original necessity for the Fund will have disappeared in your Transformation. These steps can take between 6 months up to 1 year.

        1

        You read the Become Money website

        You carry old thoughtware about money, about your Value, about Abundance and Creation. Reading the Become Money is an essential step to start noticing how old and obsolete your thoughtware about money and value is, and how it is blocking you from entering Archiarchy as a Woman of Earth.

        becomemoney.mystrikingly.com

        2

        You enter the Inner Structure

        Possibility Team

        Living in scarcity of money is a clear symptom of a 'weak' / 'unstable' Inner Structure - the shape of the trellis onto which new matrix (distinctions & experiences) can hold on to for your Being to grow.

        Clearing up your financial territory is part of Inner Structure work.

        innerstructure.mystrikingly.com

        3

        Your Build a Scarcity-Healing Team

        That is a 3Cell, 3 people that you are accountable to report your efforts, processes, fears, questions and decisions to make about money. So often, you have not been shown how to be responsible for resources: how to source the resources and how to let them flow through you.

        This 3Cell is a training team about resources that you did not receive.

        4

        You can Be Radically Honest about Your Finances

        With your 3Cell, be radically honest about your financial territory: how much do you have on your bank accounts, how much debt do you have, how much overhead do you have per month, how much money do you generate and how?

        5

        You have done the 27 Experiments to make 1000€

        Follow the exact instructions for making a 1000€ for your Path of Evolution at https://becomemoney.mystrikingly.com/#27-ways-to-make-1000

        6

        You participate in Chapter 0 of Gremlin Transformation

        Living in scarcity of anything is Gremlin Food. There is purpose to feed your Gremlin scarcity. Find out what the purpose and the ecstasy of staying in Low Drama with Life. Chapter 0 is the beginning of that journey. The journey never ends.

        gremlintransformation.mystrikingly.com

        7

        You Deliver Your Nonmaterial Value

        Not in exchange of money in the beginning. Just start delivering Nonmaterial Value, you already are Value.

        • Answer people requests for Basic Inner Navigation Skill practices and Emotional Healing Processes in the PM Healing Village
        • Participate in Heart Gym and provide unreasonable amount of value to whoever is there
        • Participate in Women's Possibility Team and offer to hold space for the experiments that turn you on
        • Deliver The Menu in your town

         

        8

        You Apply To The Women of Earth Fund

        Applicationa for the Women of Earth Fund are made to Lisa Ommert, Anne-Chloé Destremau or Vera Franco.

        As previously states, the Fund is for specific needs: a project or training. The Flow Proposal is made of:

        1. A clear ask for a sum of money to Flow towards you within a certain period of time, and the details of what the money will be used for;
        2. A transparent Excel sheet where you keep track of the Flow received from each Woman and your Flow Back;
        3. A clear plan about how you plan to FlowBack the money to the Flow Givers: how are you building a stable income?
