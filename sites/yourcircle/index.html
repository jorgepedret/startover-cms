  • A Circle Has No End

    A circle has no end...

    but it does have a beginning.

    Your circle starts when you make a database and name it your Circle.

    WHERE IS YOUR CIRCLE?

    Your Circle is in a database.

    Your database.

    Your database lists people's names, email addresses, and phone numbers.

    If someone is not in your database they are probably not in your Circle.

    Where do your Circle people come from? They come from meeting you and wanting to stay in touch with you to learn about what you are learning about. They want to be part of your journey. This means it is important to keep doing High Level Fun things where you can meet like-minded people. That's not so bad, is it?

    MEETING PEOPLE HAS NOTHING TO DO WITH FACEBOOK OR INSTAGRAM.

    Meeting people happens in person, physically, face to face, not online in social media.

    If you do not believe me about this then you will be shocked and possibly disappointed when no one shows up at the public talk you advertised as a Facebook Event even though 40 people clicked 'Going' or 'Interested'.

    The number of Facebook clickers who actually show up at physical (and even online) events can be counted on the fingers of one foot.

    Instead of spending your time making Facebook events and posting there, go around and connect with flesh and blood people, face to face (See YOUCIRCL.03 Experiment). Talk to people. Ask questions. Be curious. Build Bridges (see Connect with Other Circles).

    WHERE IS YOUR DATABASE?

    In an EXCEL sheet. In your computer.

    It takes 5 minutes to set up a database in Excel (or Google sheet).

    If I can make a database, you can make a database.

    You will feed this simple database into a free online newsletter generating program (like MailChimp, see below for the steps).

    YOUR MAIN JOBS?

    Your main jobs are:

    1. To feed your Circle (See FEED YOUR CIRCLE)

    It is more effective not to feed your Circle very much, yet it is important to feed your Circle regularly and with quality food.

    A simple monthly newsletter from you is sufficient to feed your Circle and keep them healthy and strong.

    2. To keep growing your Circle (See BUILD YOUR CIRCLE)

    Your Circle is not going to grow by itself. It is like a fragile plant, you give it water, sun and time, and then it will grow.

    The Water for your Circle is your legend-making actions and offers.

    The Sun for your Circle is your Attention and your persistence to add every name and email address to your database of the people crossing your journey or walking with you on your journey.

    A Circle requires Love, Attention, Energy.

  • SELLING TRANSFORMATION

    Legend-Making and Not Marketing

    'Selling' transformation is a very different business than selling any kind of other services or products. To acquire most other services or products only one thing is required in exchange: your money. The job people selling ordinary services and products is to convince you that you better spend your money on their products and services than their competitors' products and services. This is Marketing 101. There are all sorts of techniques for this. There are entire Masters degree to learn how to brainwash and convince clients that your product is the best.

    Selling Transformation is not the business of convincing clients that they should get transformation. The main reason is that it doesn't work. Before your clients are your clients, they are identified with their Box. The Box does not want transformation, the Box will do anything to avoid that your potential client gets any idea that transformation is possible. The Box thinks that if it can stay the same than it will survive, and the person inside of the Box will survive too.

    'Acquiring' transformation requires the highest cost imaginable: the life of the Box. The Box - as it is - will not survive transformation. Your job is then to DEMONSTRATE something that the client will find more valuable than the life of their Box.

    Demonstrating that thing so that your client wants more of it in their life is NOT about explaining. If you explain it to them, they could probably understand at an intellectual level what you mean and they might find it interesting, but they will not be ready to pay you to take them through the process. Explaining hinders your circle building. You might have had this experience already.

    Explaining and convincing are old technology. There are newer technology: creating a vacuum.

    Demonstrating by creating a vacuum for your client has the possibility of opening a door for your client in which they can see that they didn't know they didn't know about something.

    Demonstrating by vacuum happens in many ways: Become Integrity, Make Legends and Cause Adventure.

    Having Integrity...

    ... with the work that you deliver. Walk your talk.

    For example, if you deliver Workshops and Trainings to heal emotional reactivity, next time you are having an emotional reaction in a group, admit it and tell them that you will go do the emotional healing process so that you can keep having this conversation with them.

    For example, if you deliver Workshops and Trainings for people to learn to keep their center, demonstrate that you can keep your center around them. Stop being adaptive, don't allow Gremlin to take over a space of relationship. Walk your talk.

    For example, if you deliver Workshops and Trainings in which you will distinguish parts and purposes, practice having Meta-Conversation about parts and purposes without judgments.

    Legend-Making actions...

    ... are actions that sends an energetic vibrations in the field that is strong enough to disrupt the status quo.

    Those energetic vibrations are ripples, identical to the ripples in water after you throw in a stone. When the ripples hit hard matter (like the edges of a swimming pool) they come back to the sender: YOU.

    The hard matter in the energetic fields are your clients: the people who have enough matrix to sense the vibrations and to respond to them. Your legend-making actions and their ripples will be invisible to most of the population on the Planet. You could consider this a good news, this means that the people coming back to you want something from you instead of wanting to kill you. The more you stay invisible to the fanatics of modern culture, the more you can keep doing your job in peace.

    Legend-Making actions are actions that open doors into new territory that were invisible to people before.

    Demonstrating emotional healing on radio show is legend-making, committing to deliver a work-talk every week is legend-making, holding space for 3/3/3 is legend-making, giving free talks in book shops is legend-making, walking barefoot everywhere is legend-making, going around the world using no fossil fuel powered engine machines is legend-making.

    Let your legends do the impossible Magic of opening the difficult Doors so you can use your time and energy actually delivering the services of your Archetypal Lineage.

    Truly adventurous territories are inner and relational. Conscious Adventure is Transformational.

    Practice becoming someone who can cause Adventure, and you will attract true adventurers!

    Adventure is available at any moment. Adventure is going into uncharted territory together. Where? How?

    Navigating the territories of Survival Strategies, Emotional Reactivity, Gremlin habits, and Box reactions can bring immense new awarenesses and celebrations. But, that is not all that there is... The purpose of digging through the mud is to get to the Light. How well can you Cause Adventure of new Possibility, new options to Choose from, new ways to Connection, NonLinear Life Plan, new Gameplans, new Gameworlds, new Nonmaterial Value for other people?

    Committing to someone else's true Commitment is an Adventure.

    A STORY FROM LISA-MARIA GOERTZ OF SOMOS, ON TENERIFE ISLAND

    Lisa-Maria Goertz, 02 June 2025

    https://somos.land/en

    https://aridani.info

    Dear Evolutionary Team,

    This weekend I went to a gathering in the neighbouring island of Gran Canaria and a lot of things happened and I want to share with you what I have been discovering.

    The last 2 years I have become more and more selective about where I goo and where I spend my time, to the point where I have created a bubble for myself at SOMOS. Here, I say how it goes, I create the reality II want and invite people into it, here there is no drugs no alcohol, we use the distinctions, we feel. Most of the time I am holding space and this creates a certain effect in me, a kind of comfortability, here I am received, people who come want what I have to offer.

    And I love what we are creating here, it's what I want AND I don't love the way I've been shielding myself or withholding myself from new experiences and adventures, and protecting myself from the unknown.

    I have been using stories and judgements to justify not going to certain places and doing certain things. Here there will be boom boom music, I wont go, here people might be smoking weed and drinking alcohol and there's nothing there for me, here it’s cool land and people don’t really want to feel, I won’t be able to relate with them – I can find all this evidence, that people don't want to do the work, that they don't want to feel, people are doing fake Evolution selling consciousness and pretending to be spiritual and sharing it on Instagram etc. This is my victim story, why don't more people come and show-up in what I am creating here? Why are they so unwilling to really evolve? These are my victim questions.

    This weekend I challenged many of those stories, this weekend I let E.C.C.O. move me (Earth Coincidence Control Office).

    The man who came to do the barro floor invited me to his community in Gran Canaria. In this valley there are four different projects and they have created a joint community and this weekend they had a weekend gathering where everyone was offering their no material value in different workshops, shared meals a jam session and on Saturday night a DJ session. When I got the invitation something inside me said GO. Even though I was scared about the music, the drugs, even though I was scared about being a fish out of water. Even though my stories were pulling me to stay in the bubble, I listened to the call and I went.

    And it was deeply enlivening, connecting, archetypal, nourishing in my 5 Bodies and ecstatic high-level fun.

    The land is beautiful, like an oasis in the desert in a deep ravine full of palm trees - they call it the daughters of the palm trees, it's by a necropolis which is where all the indigenous people were buried and so it's a very sacred land which they really revere and honor. The spaceholders of the place say it is a very feminine space which invited people to see their shadows and hit bottom.

    I connected with a group of women there who from the onset were so open and vulnerable and welcoming. By the time I left they was a sense of sisterhood that I have only before felt in the WOE bridgehouse and labs and in the WOE possibility team. They are women of earth.

    I'm realizing now being in a place where my value, where who I am was so fully received, how painful it is when people don't receive my value, when they block it. And to avoid the pain of this I've been making these stories that keep me seperate, this is what I see now.

    This weekend E.C.C.O led me on a journey of really being celebrated. These women were so up for the experiments that I proposed, we did I feel fear relating with you because as a group and they were so dangerous and vulnerable. Some of them had been friends for a long while and they bought things to the surface that were really painful and they cried and when one woman was crying and they were complimenting her and trying to negate her sadness, I landed the distinction of rescuing and it landed immediately and they were really grateful and touched.

    On the first morning I was walking around this beautiful land and suddenly I heard women raging, it felt like a call to my being, I went looking for the source of the screaming and it was the same women, I said I want to I want to feel with you and then we raged and screamed and laughed together. The woman who had started the screaming had done so because she had seen a man bullying his son and had stood up to him and felt so much anger about a child being treated in that way and so much fear about speaking out.

    Later on one of the women came over to me and she said can I share something with you, she said when I really start to be who I am, to stand in my power and my bigness I feel so guilty, I feel so much shame, and I don't think it's just mine, I feel like I'm carrying it for many generations of women past. And she burst out into tears and asked me if I would go to work with her. I listened and I allowed her to speak, I gave her completion loops and it unfolded something in her. She said she'd never been able to speak about these things with anyone, she said she'd never been able to open up in this way, that she hasn’t experienced being listened to like this before. And as the boom boom music played 200 m away at 1:00 in the morning where yes some people were smoking weed and taking drugs, me and this woman of Earth stood under the palm trees under the stars and we cried together and we raged together, we shared our pain, we went liquid together and laughed like hyenas and shared such raw terrifying intimacy.

    Before this I had this incredible conversation with a 22-year-old man who has this vision to connect ecosystems of eco-villages to start building a new system. As we were speaking to each other, both of our archetypal bodies were visibly vibrating and we were feeling so much joy and surprise (fear) - he said he could see this vision of a giant stood behind him as we spoke, he said he has seen this image before, it sounded like his Archetypal lineage. He said he often has visions and got messages from source.

    This 22 year old man was talking openly about his fears, about what was holding him back, he was so vulnerable and so insane in terms of modern culture and so out there. He wants to come with his partner to Aridani and SOMOS, we are setting up a call.

    I also shared a deep resonance with another woman who spent the last 20 years traveling, travelled around the world on a motorbike, been in jail,helped buy land in the amazon to protect it, made millions, rubbed shoulders with the richest and poorest.

    She has spent years interviewing women from all walks of life, indigenous women, multi-millionaires and is making a documentary, among many other projects. She is an incredible experimenter who has become embittered with modern culture and is searching for a life that makes sense. We were so excited to meet each other and both felt a longing to collaborate with each other and now we're in touch. I told her about this new idea I had of creating this regenerative sponsorship/fundraising platform (news soon to come) and she was so on my team, she asked me questions that bought a lot of clarity and allowed me to unfold more.

    She is also interested in joining Aridani and might come this weekend to make the cob floor. Even if I never see her again, I am delighted to have met her.

    The whole weekend was indeed a delight for my archetypal body and what I get is that what changed was me, that something shifted to allow me to have those experiences, to relate to my reality in a new way where I saw doorways instead of dead ends. It wasn’t a stroke of luck, the filter through which I saw reality was different and that made my reality different, it made my being a magnet.

    I want to share this story because I realized that I've been withdrawing from the world in a way, and I feel sad about that, I feel sad about giving up on people.

    I sense that by being in Israel for a month and doing a lot of experiments, going to a lot of events, being around a lot of different people, challenging many stories I had, it opened something up in me.

    This does not mean I am going to start going to events all the time or hanging around in cool land parties looking for something.

    And I do declare that I am absurdly movable for E.C.C.O. That I am available to listen to my impulses and decide to use my fear to guide me, this is how I want to create my culture, this is how I find my team, my circle.

    Hiding in my castle feeling sorry for myself because sharing flyers on Facebook and telegram and Instagram isn’t working - doesn't work for me!

    I've been trying to find the easy way, the hiding way, wanting people to come to me so I didn’t have to face my pain, the pain of how it is, the fear of meeting people where they are without losing my culture.

    Having this experience of being such a gift to these women, and feeling so nourished by those interactions and them being such a gift to me, I am in this liquid state of rewriting stories and allowing myself to really be in the unknown, to allow life to be the adventure that it is. That is how I want to move in the world, this is how I want to be my culture.

    Love,

    Lisa-Maria
