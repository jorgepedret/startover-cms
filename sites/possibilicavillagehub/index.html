        • What We Do

          Possibilica Village Hub is a Gaian Gameworld. We are part of a Global Network of Empowered Citizens and Communities for Long-term Sustainability.

          These are our key Bright Principles that guide our actions:

          Radical Responsibility

          Possibilica Village Hub is rooted in Radical Responsibility and the Thoughtware of Possibility Management.

          Evolution

          Evolution is a continuous Transformational Process and involves circling on the Learning Spiral with 15 seconds of applause before becoming conscious of your next unconscious incompetence. The Adulthood Path of evolution includes an endless series of Liquid States.

          Healing

          We are committed to 3-phase healing. It is about removing you from the illness rather than trying to remove the illness from you.

          Archearchy

          Next culture - Archearchy - is the culture that is naturally emerging now that matriarchy and patriarchy have run their course. It is a culture of Initiated collaborating adult women and men.

          Love

          Archetypal Love is the source that feeds Archearchy. The experience and expression of Archetypal Love is Archeamory. As we explore and expand each person's capacity to source Archetypal Love, our ability to relate evolves.

        • "I can feel the wind at my back. It feels like my archetypal lineage floodgates have opened."

          Jo Newton

        • We are creating a living community of real people, finding out how it goes to radically own our evolution, taking radical responsibility for life.

          By acknowledging that the current systems are no longer sustaining life, this Village Hub is a place to heal, transform and become initiated into fully alive, responsible adults.

          Contrary to how it has been sold, Adulthood does not have to be a burden to bear. Rather, it can be limitless, fun and expansive. One cannot evolve alone – it takes a village to become initiated into adulthood.

           

          Using the tools of Possibility Management, Possibilica Village Hub brings to life the possibility that each human has a part to play in the creation of next culture, the culture after matriarchy and patriarchy have run their course.

           

          We physically live in a space with a group of other human beings committed to the same context of Radical Responsibility. It is a living experiment in which we explore questions such as:

          “How do I foster a way of life that fully nurtures human aliveness?"

          "How do I tend to my natural tendency to evolve beyond reason?”

          “How does it really go to live in next culture all the time?”

           

          Living in Possibilica Village Hub is evolutionary fuel for all inhabitants. By holding workshops, trainings, and events, both on the land and beyond, the fruit of this evolution are shared with the wider community. Inevitably the participation in such events feeds back to fuel the co-evolution of the Village Hub also.

           

          Possibilica Village Hub nestles on the land of Waitorupai ("three good waters", reflecting the three springs in the valley). It is intended for others to come and experience a living model of Radical Responsibility. They leave with seeds to carry into their own culture, community and inspiration for creating their own Next Culture Village.

           

          Possibilica Village Hub serves human beings from all over the world to recharge and take flight, fuelling projects, ideas and creative next steps; carrying the reference point in all five bodies that something completely different is possible right now.

        • WHERE WE ARE

          We are in the North Island of Aotearoa, New Zealand. We've experienced an evolutionary rocket moment which means we are letting go of Waitorupai as a base for our Village. The next is yet to emerge. One things is clear, however:
          As we live in Archearchy, the land that we live on owns us. Our main question in being responsible for the land is: "How can I make the land better in its own terms? My job is to help the land do what the land wants to do, in the way the land wants to do it, only better."
          Derrick Jensen, https://youtu.be/E0Ii2p57AlQ

          Preamble

          Radical Responsibility is not and never can be a set of rules or laws to live by - as that automatically removes the own authority. Rather it is a possible avenue to walk down, tasting the air and touching the leaves to experience a new way of interacting with the world. In this way it has a quality of sensitivity - a gentle awareness and spaciousness.
