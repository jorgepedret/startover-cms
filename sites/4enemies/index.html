  • "Know thine enemies, know thy selves."

    - Old Possibilitator Proverb

  • The Path Of The Possibilitator

    Responsibility is applied consciousness... responsibility is consciousness in action.

    There is no good or bad, better or worse, right or wrong place to be on your Path.

    The interesting question is: Are you excellently being where you are?

    ----------------------------------------------

    WARNING:

    Some of what is written below may make sense to you. Some of it may not.

    That is the whole point.

    Where you are is indicated by what makes sense.

    You are not meant to make sense of it all.

    We suggest that you use what is useful to you now.

    Then later, when you start feeling uncertain again, come back here and start reading from the beginning.

    Different sections will make a new kind of sense to you then.

    You will find where you are, and it will probably be at a different place.

    Remember: no place is better or worse than any other place on the Sacred Mountain.

    Spaceholders are needed at every place.

    You won't move up the Mountain until you Replace Yourself at your current post.

    That is the value of making it a custom in your Team to ongoingly train up each other as best you can.

    This website hopes to help you be more excellently where you are.

    While ‘climbing’ the 'Sacred Mountain' of personal transformation and awareness-skill-building, you may occasionally rely on information from a map made by explorers who have gone before you to find out where you are. Distinctions that they report from their research and documentation efforts can, in the right moment, be invaluable.

     

    Yet, there are as many maps of the Sacred Mountain as there are Paths.

     

    One map was documented by Carlos Castaneda as given to him by his teacher Don Juan Matus. We found it in the book: The Teachings Of Don Juan: A Yaqui Way Of Knowledge, by Carlos Castaneda, pp 82-87, Penguin Books, London, England, 1970. (NOTE: In other editions the pages are different. E.g. in the Washington Square Press Reissue edition (March 3, 1985) the pages are pp 53-57, and pp 158.) We want you to know that in applying the '4 Enemies' map to the Path of a Possibilitator we have unfolded it beyond Mr. Castaneda's original explanations.

     

    We offer you our understanding of this map because it explains relationships between a Possibilitator and her practice in a way that makes alchemical sense to us.

     

    Your practice is most effective when you practice according to where you are, in other words, when you practice according to which domain of the Mountain you are exploring. This map distinguishes 4 unique domains.

     

    As we see it, this map may be most useful to those Possibilitators regarding themselves as an apprentice to Alchemy.

     

    Progress along this particular Path of self-discovery and transformation emerges through successful encounters with conditions and forces that Don Juan Matus formulates as 'defeating the 4 natural enemies of a wo(man) on the Path'.

     

    Defeating one enemy automatically squeezes you up the Mountain such that you enter the domain of your next enemy.

     

    Each domain up the Sacred Mountain builds upon and supersedes the previous domain.

     

    When you, a Possibilitator - an apprentice to Alchemy - first enter a domain, you have one relationship to that domain's context and possibilities. Over time and with right efforts, your relationship changes in unexpected ways. What at first appears to be a Golden Key opening a Doorway into a new domain, eventually becomes a trap from which the apprentice to Alchemy must then escape through sophistication of awareness into the entry way of the next domain, which itself then becomes a trap.

     

    And so it goes, eh?

     

    At the same time, this website proposes a new use for a map. Instead of using this map to go somewhere else, use this map for being more excellently where you are.

     

    The journey of a Possibilitator is archetypal. Go to the archetypal level of where you are.

     

    Merely through entertaining the concept that there might exist a bridge from where you are to someplace else confronts you with your first enemy: Fear.

  • Your First Enemy : Fear

    "Off with their heads!" commanded the Queen of Hearts.

    Up to that moment you realize there might be something beyond the limits of your present reality, your psychological defense strategy has functioned to protect you from exactly this knowledge. "There is no edge," it tell you, "so of course there can exist no bridges to somewhere else!"

     

    The ostrich strategy has worked so far.

     

    Your current view of reality is known to provide sufficient safety for your survival because the fact of the matter is: you have so far survived.

     

    Any change in your strategy throws the entire equation into unknown circumstances, which your psychology relegates to the category of 'a very bad idea'. Seeking change opens you up to the possibility that you might make a fatal mistake.

     

    Fear stops you from trying anything new like poison spray stops an ant.

     

    Even if you are enthusiastic about learning something new, as soon as you begin making gestures in that direction, the learning process and the learning itself are so different from what you fantasized that it might be that you immediately mistrust yourself for deciding to learn.

     

    Or else you mistrust the experience of learning, being certain that it will not succeed, and you become afraid.

     

    More afraid than you ever thought possible.

     

    How could so much be so new so suddenly, and so contrary to your expectations?

     

    After all these years in school, listening to your parents, teachers, and preachers... after so long being alive in the modern world... how could you know so little?

     

    How could learning new things be so difficult?

     

    How could seeing new things feel so dangerous?

     

    The challenge of facing your fears becomes overwhelming. Your once bold adventurous purposes become a boxing ring for a fight to the death between ‘Dr. Yes’ and ‘Dr. No.’

     

    Fear reigns. You are stymied at every turn. None of your ordinary friends even knows what you are talking about.

     

    But if you give up and try to head back into ordinary sanity, ordinary life, peace and safety, the game is over and your enemy Fear has won.

     

    Your impulses to discovery are thrashed by your scattered attention and wild confusion. You settle back into your normal routines and try to forget about such craziness, at some point realizing that this entire process just described is often played out within your unconscious.

     

    On the outside you may simply look like you are procrastinating on your Path of personal development, or like you have simply lost interest and are attracted to some other pursuit. But make no mistake, on the inside Fear has stopped you cold. This description is real and exact.

     

    Nothing can aid you to defeat and overcome Fear because Fear is undeniable and will never go away.

     

    Facing your direct experiences of Fear is unaccountably daunting.

     

    Working with Fear is intimidating because so few have confronted their own personal Fear and know how it goes. Yet this is what starts the whole process of evolution. Fear work initiates everything.

     

    To proceed as a Possibilitator you must at some point in your life become frustrated enough with Fear to say, “This stuckness is too painful for me. I am willing to face Fear directly. I am at the choice point. Either I stay as I am and continue being dead, or I face Fear even if it feels like I will die.”

     

    Only by lowering your Numbness Bar to Fear do you acquire enough authentic necessity to draw down catalytic influences from the next domain up the Sacred Mountain. Your paradox is paradoctored!

     

    Brief glimpses of new possibilities flash into your mind. Somehow a distinction lands in your 5 Bodies.

     

    It becomes tacitly clear that you cannot conquer Fear through force of will or denial, because the way out of the grips of Fear is through Fear itself!

     

    How astonishing! You do some experiments of feeling afraid and not fighting it, simply staying in Fear letting the screams come out as loud as possible. You keep your eyes open and go ahead anyway, even as you tremble, whimper, and sweat.

     

    After repeatedly choosing to stay conscious and Present in the midst of Fear you discover how to re-wire your mind.

     

    All along you have had the 'red wire' connected to the 'blue wire' in your mind. You had it wired that the experience of fear has the meaning: “This is bad. Something is very wrong. I am broken. This is dangerous. I am going to die.”

     

    After the Self Surgery of re-wiring your brain, you make it so that the 'red wire' is now connected to the 'red wire'. Then the experience of fear has the meaning: ‘Fear’.

     

    Suddenly the entire universe of Fear collapses into one simple profound bit of clarity: Fear is fear.

     

    That was the totality of your life-long problem with Fear: you had it cross-wired.

     

    IMPORTANT NOTE: If your self-surgery is successful, you will feel outrage. You will feel grief. You must fully and out-loud experience and express these emotions consciously to people who are able to listen to your full intensity emotions. This integration process is necessary to complete the new wiring.

     

    Your tiny new bit of Clarity solves your Fear problem in an instant!

     

    You start to feel surer of yourself. Fear does not go away, of course. But it does not have to go away. After your rewiring surgery, Fear no longer owns you.

     

    You are now free to feel afraid!

     

    Suddenly you get your aliveness back!

     

    Your relationship to Fear was transformed through the rewiring. Clarity was the key!

     

    Your Fear of gaining Radical Knowledge (as in: Specific Practical Applications of Radical Knowledge, in other words: S.P.A.R.K.s) no longer stops you from gaining Radical Knowledge! The Path shines before you like a stroll on the strand!

     

    You begin to get the notion that Clarity has allowed you to conquer your greatest enemy (Fear!) and the way up the Sacred Mountain seems free of obstacles.

     

    Clarity has become your ally, your secret weapon. You begin collecting Distinctions like a hungry man fills his shopping cart in a grocery store. Your bookshelf grows heavy with transformational titles.

     

    Your mind is blown wide open. The Adventure begins. The game is afoot!

     

    Clarity delivers distinctions that inform you how Fear is only a feeling, one of the 4 Feelings: anger, sadness, fear, and joy! Clarity explains how there is a difference between the 4 Feelings and the 4 Emotions! Feelings and Emotions are both useful, but for entirely different things. Feelings are for handling things! Emotions are for healing things!

     

    Clarity provides new thoughtmaps in which it is okay to feel without being a victim of your Feelings. In fact, your Feelings and your Emotions start serving you professionally!

     

    Clarity allows you to discover how to calibrate your Feelings Detector so as to help others also learn to Inner Navigate their Feelings and Emotions. Your Team can now help each other go through their own Emotional Healing Processes.

     

    Feelings and Emotions used to be so important to you. Now these experiences are merely sensations. Clarity itself reveals that feelings are triggered by stories you create to self-define and to hold the world still. Now you can create feelings on purpose - Conscious Feelings - to serve your Destiny.

     

    You can reinvent yourself so as to reinvent the world.

     

    You have learned how to learn and can anticipate the Liquid State before it occurs, welcoming it boldly as a signal that you are bringing in new Clarity.

     

    Technologies for solving human interaction problems and creating new realities become available to you like an open box of tools, and you learn to use them at home and at work, internally and externally.

     

    As you distinguish your own purposes, the purposes of others become obvious. Upon this empathy is built the castle of understanding, patience, kindness, and ultimately compassion.

     

    You are no longer hooked by other people's games. You are no longer a victim. You can do anything you please. Every option is an option. The world seems radiantly bright, clear and sharp. You can Become Present in it.

     

    Nothing is concealed. You think, “Clarity is fantastic!” And it is. Clarity is a sparkling gateway into a magical kingdom where you can pull out your magic wand and Declare, “Something completely different from this is possible right now,” and sure enough, there is new Possibility.

     

    Whole new Doorways appear and you gain immediate access to unlimited Spaces through Clarity.

     

    Clarity is the best.

     

    Clarity is gold.

     

    Clarity is the way.

     

    Tremendous joy arises as Clarity becomes yours.

  • Your Second Enemy : Clarity

    A distinction gains its value from its context.

    Who is setting your context?

    You become a Clarity generating machine.

    Everywhere you see people who do not have Clarity. Yet when you generously offer them your Clarity-developing Tools, Thoughtmaps, and Processes, they rebel, calling you manipulative, aggressive, arrogant and superior.

    People say that they come to you for help and you ‘kill’ them with your intensity.

    They are afraid of you.

    You have taken practical possession of rare Distinctions and Transformational Technologies, and have begun to value the Sword Of Clarity more than your life.

    You have made Clarity the most important thing, over and over again solving every kind of problem.

    Then one day you Observe yourself using Clarity to defend your position on the Sacred Mountain. You catch yourself preaching fanatically.

    Clarity provides answers, yes. And... answers destroy questions. Good questions are needed to create proper vacuum to draw you forward.

    Rigidity sets in, nailing your shoes to the floor. Insights which were once fresh, each one reordering reality for you in unexpected ways, have now become required. You become very attached to ‘your’ Clarity.

    You get addicted to the ego-gratifying experience of having a new insight, seeking insight for the sake of insight, as if insights were important.

    Click, click, click, click, click, personal insights cause reordering to happen... very rewarding for personality, for ego, for Gremlin.

    You experience gaining a new insight as if it is your personal achievement.

    You go from place to place to place, book to book to book, teacher to teacher to teacher, looking only for more insights, thinking that insights are the key. Your bookshelves and Beep! Books overflow with your Treasure!

    You dedicate your life to possessing ideas, collecting them, defending them. You have become your ideas. The certainty provided by infallible Clarity is so brilliant it causes blindness.

    Slowly the reality of the situation begins to seep into your awareness. Unbelievably you have encountered your second enemy: Clarity.

    How bizarre! What at first functioned for you as the harbinger of halcyon days has become a glass ceiling and now blocks your evolution.

    What at first served to help you get unstuck and to learn, now serves your Gremlin. You cannot imagine how this could happen.

    You never imagined encountering this condition.

    Try as you may, you cannot use the technologies of Clarity to solve this riddle and move ahead.

    No matter how great your frustration, Clarity will not undermine itself.

    You have climbed through the mountainous domain of Clarity and are nearing the lower borders of another domain, a domain you did not suspect existed until now, because when Clarity was King, nothing else was necessary.

    Now you have to become a poor beggar and wait attentively. Some new factor has to enter the scene, yet you have no idea what it could be.

    Your experiments become wilder. You risk failing in bigger ways.

    The feedback you give becomes more devastating and direct. You let some wobble and unpredictability enter your creations as a last resort in your battle with Clarity.

    You experience yourself as beginning to lose your mind.

    Mind is no longer enough when your authenticity is at stake.

    Your relationships hang over a knife edge.

    Without relationship you cannot do your work as an apprentice to Alchemy. Without relationship you keep killing the patient because your Sword is too swift and heavy handed. You must put your Sword Of Clarity into the hands of something other than yourself, something greater than yourself, something you do not own, cannot dominate and cannot think your way around.

    Suddenly it starts making sense why you have Bright Principles and an Archetypal Lineage.

    Blundering ahead into unknown territory, regarding the Clarity toolbox with skepticism and newfound suspicions, almost as if the Clarity were a mistake, suddenly, accidentally, a vast and broader perspective subsumes your actions.

    You are no longer disconnected from the other when you do your surgeries.

    You are moved by a force that includes the other with you indivisibly, indubitably. You experience being amazed at the unexpected gracefulness and fluidity arising as you surrender your personal certainty into the service of a Principle that is common between you and the other, a Principle that is common to both of you. You find yourself more on your Client's side than they are.

    They no longer represent an adversary to be transformed or subverted through Clarity, but rather a friend in pain, lost alone on a cold wintry night, who has taken the risk of knocking on a stranger's door, hoping to be welcomed into light and warmth, not to be told that if they had been more conscious and more responsible they would not be in this situation, and then have the door slammed in their face.

    And it works...

    And without warning you have bounced into the next domain up the Sacred Mountain, the domain of Power, a power so vast as to be undefeatable.

    Clarity is obviously not enough. Clarity comes from knowing. You need the spaciousness of NOT knowing for the Principles to function within.

    You see that Power is not a tool but a bird of a different feather: power is a creative transformational force.

    You are relieved to expand into new territory, not even imaginable before.

  • Your Third Enemy : Power

    Maybe you can, and maybe you can't, but the most relevant question is: Why would you?

    Your aliveness expands into new dimensions: You can Create new and useful results. You can call things to appear and disappear. You can Cause Transformation.

    You discover that there are two kinds of Power:

    1. The Power of Being.
    2. The Power of Doing.

    These two resources combined seem endless, and also endlessly fascinating.

    Your capacity for Presence amplifies a thousand fold. You can connect in ways never before imaginable. You can actually relate as a space of Listening.

    Your relationships blossom. They were at risk because you thought relating was a problem you could solve through Clarity. Hah! How foolish that all seems now. You discover the power of Being With.

    You think, “I learned about Feelings and Emotions. I have reference points in my 5 Bodies for Feelings energies. But Power is a formidable new intelligence informing me how relationship actually works.”

    Previously you would engage in mind-war with your partner by striking Clarity with Clarity. The result could only be breakdown because no one was calling forth the Principles of Relationship. Now you can live related.

    You are impressed with what you know, but the power of Principles shows up in a space of not-knowing.

    Relationship is not just male/female. Relationship is a vast space of Principles and Transformations which falls into breakdown at the level of insight and Clarity, staying in breakdown until you move up the Mountain into the powerful fluidity of Principles.
     

    You see the fallacy of trying to solve conflicts from the domain of Clarity. There are Principles at work: ‘the Feminine has Power in everything’ and ‘the Masculine has Power in nothing’. The Principles of Masculinity and Femininity come alive in the domain of Power.

    You use Power, surf on it, direct it to take you anywhere, but you do not own it. You get all the power you need, but it is not your Power.

    Principles cannot be owned. Principles are the many faces of God. Principles exist beyond human life. The Power of consciousness becomes your ally, and you move as E.C.C.O. directs. You and E.C.C.O. are allies in the Universe.

    Clarity does not go away when you enter the domain of Power. Your context has simply expanded and Clarity is now seen for the tight minimum Sword-reality that it functions within. You have additional dimensions of influence now. Your network of powerful influencers has grown global, perhaps even intergalactic.

    Yes, Clarity works in the domain of Clarity. But now you have a greater domain to explore called Power. And in this domain, reality is non-linear.

    Everything is possible. Nothing is a barrier. All can be transformed into something else, as you please.

    You take care to keep yourself in line at all times, to live a Life of Practice, and Power functions through you. What a pleasure. What a servant you are.

    You think, “Well, I've got power now. I can be fluid and Flow Power wherever it is needed and wanted. I can call a new Principle into a chamber and it harmonizes, balances, and heals things.”

    You sense how this Power is significant, and cannot help but thinking that because Power serves you it is 'your' Power.

    You imagine yourself to be a Wizard or Sorceress, ordering the universe around to satisfy the Principles. You start making rules and systems, building and transforming gameworlds. And you start to think there is nothing to worry about – ever.

    You forget how temporary such a condition might be. You secretly imagine that you are immortal, a co-creator with god Herself.

    Oh, dear! Not again! It rears its ugly head again?

    Your Gremlin's furry little devilish face appears, grinning slyly, and again hijacks your capability within a domain.

    At first, in the domain of Power, you had the innocence, caution, and wakefulness of a beginner. As you became more and more familiar with its function, you made assumptions. You nodded off to sleep. And in those increasingly common moments, the Gremlin turns Power towards serving its own autonomous purposes.

    You recall that when Clarity pulled you up out of the darkness of Fear you were so overjoyed to discover an entirely new domain where Fear was no longer King. Clarity arrived so delightfully, so refreshing.

    You remember how when Clarity - which had at first saved you, and then evolved into your betrayer, your prison, your second enemy - you were nearly devastated.

    Your longing and desire to gain more Clarity were almost rubbed out forever. Until you encountered the next domain, the domain of Power, and you were saved.

    Now it is Power that has become your enemy. Its spaciousness is too relaxed.

    Having Power erases the urgency of attentiveness and respect. It gives you no qualifiers, no challenges. You became too familiar with having everything work out your way, too addicted to controlling outcomes.

    You reach the upper range of the domain of Power. But how could there be anything else? What could be further up the mountain than Power? More powerful than Power?

    It feels so humbling to become a beggar again, to try to defeat your new enemy with weakness. But there is nothing left for you...

  • Your Fourth Enemy : Old Age

    At age twenty we worry what others think of us.At age forty we don't care what others think of us.At age sixty we discover they haven't been thinking of us at all.

    By now the bones creak, the joints ache.

    Journeying up the Sacred Mountain takes a long time and every ounce of energy. The next step on the Path lies in front of you, yet you are blocked and troubled.

    You glance in the mirror and see wrinkles where before there was smoothness, a sagging butt and breasts where before there was perkiness and bounce. You don't move so fast.

    Time ticks away. You have gotten older.

    After all that you have been through, how could you be lost again, begging for a way out of the tyranny of Power?

    Power is not enough. Causing things to happen has lost it meaning and appeal. Raw creation... cannot take you further. None of the tricks work. How could they? They are tricks!

    You seek something more significant, more transcendent and impersonal than Power.

    In rare private moments you catch a quick glimpse of dark hollow eyes staring at you from the shadows.

    Those eyes have been stalking you with their chilling image your whole life.

    That familiar countenance... What is it?

    It has a name: It is Death.

    Death paces just behind your left shoulder like a vulture waiting to feed, wishing to consume everything you have worked so hard to fashion into a functioning whole.

    Death wants to eat your life.

    It is hungry, ready to pounce upon you at any incautious move. Even crossing the street or going down stairs becomes perilous.

    In the tension of this ever-present awareness, your abundant spaciousness suddenly vanishes and a new urgency arises. The imminent presence of Death squeezes you reflexively up the Mountain into the next domain: Old Age.

    The wisdom of limits inherent in Old Age reorders your Aliveness, your Clarity, and your Power into a new level of coordinated alertness.

    Old Age is a new factor, a new force, a new guide.

    Old Age knows the timing of things. It knows imminence. It knows compassion. Once again you work with diligence.

    Old Age knows that there is no place in any of the four domains (Fear, Clarity, Power, or Old Age) that is safe. You can lose your Work in a moment at any altitude on the Sacred Mountain. One slip and you tumble down past decades of Work.

    The wisdom of Old Age also knows that you can never conquer the enemy form of Old Age, because only Death in the end can prevail.

    The battle rages.

    Your crusty but powerful Gremlin incessantly squeaks whining demands and justifications for procrastination, for easing your pace, for taking private time after all these years. He purrs, "Let things take care of themselves. Have a rest. Lie down and relax, forget it all for a while. You deserve a break today. Everyone will understand. There is nothing more you can do. You are so feeble and so old.”

    Old Age defeats Power - your last enemy - defeats Gremlin itself - with Presence.

    In a few private moments you acknowledge to yourself that you have not retreated into complacency, or surrendered to mere comfort. You have retained your aliveness, clarity, presence, kindness, generosity, and perhaps also compassion, until the end... until your last breath. Here you are.

    You have guarded the transformational Path, and have thusly given it Integrity.

  • Reality: Evolution Is More Complex Than A Mountain

    There are many Paths up the Mountain, but there is no Mountain.

     

    In reality, there is no top end in any of the 4 domains.

     

    Try to picture a Sacred Quadruple Helix

    Using a Sacred Mountain as a metaphor for the Path of the evolution of consciousness is sadly inadequate.

     

    Reality is far more complex than this.

     

    Nonetheless, there are many practical reasons to begin with imagining the evolutionary Path in connection with a Sacred Mountain. For example:

    • The Sacred Mountain is beyond the city limits. By remaining in the city as a 'city dweller' you will never even reach the edge of your culture to discover how much more there is to reality. The Path up the Sacred Mountain begins far beyond the city limits. The city and the Sacred Mountain are two entirely different worlds. You can develop skills to live in both worlds - psyche and Essence, personality and Being - but learning evolutionary mountain climbing will change the way you live in the city, forever.
    • Going on expeditions towards the mountain, setting up base camps, shifting out of the city dweller culture into the evolutionary mountain climbing culture is all Team work. Without successfully creating your Team, without learning how to efficiently liberate and navigate circular group intelligence, you will not get very far towards the mountain. In a city you can live as a recluse, never having to truly connect with or depend on anyone. You can isolate yourself your whole city life. You cannot isolate yourself on the path of evolution of consciousness. Everything fake or neurotic is visible, and everything real is required.
    • In a city you have a basement, a cellar, a car with a glove compartment, perhaps also an office. Each is full of drawers, cupboards, closets, cabinets. In the city you can easily suffocate your Being under your collection of material objects, and hardly even notice. Everyone else is doing the same thing. Over the edge, on the Path, every item you own must be carried on your own back. You get one book, one knife, one pair of shoes, or you learn to go barefoot. Everything unnecessary soon falls away as exorbitant excess. Unconscious emotional baggage on the Path becomes obvious very quickly. Each bit you heal or let go of calls for celebration.
    • In the city, there are many rules, and many loopholes around those rules. City rules are loose enough to allow all the myriads of neurosis and psychosis to co-exist without immediately killing each other. Social masks prevail. On the road towards the Sacred Mountain, fewer rules apply, but they apply ever more strictly. City regulations are sloppy. You do not get fined or put in prison every time you cross the street outside of the crosswalk lines. Further up the mountain, one false step or a poorly tied knot and you easily tumble down the cliff. More attentiveness and forethought builds the required awareness.
    • And so on.
    In the Sacred Mountain metaphor, the mountain itself has a top end. From far off you can see it. None of this matches the Patch of the evolution of consciousness. From nowhere on the Patch can you see a top end. In fact, each of the so-called '4 Enemies' has in its domain no top end. For example, after 45 years of research we are still now being blown away by the depth and value of wisdom and useful applications to be derived from conscious Fear. The domain of Fear has no top end.
     
    This proves to be true also of the domain of Clarity, the domain of Power, and the domain of Old Age. None of the 4 domains has a top end, no bottom end, no limiting breadth or width or depth or time, no final peak or horizon to conquer or possess. The evolution of consciousness occurs in unlimited domains.
     
    In addition to that, the journey of discovery along any path in each of these 4 domains goes along in a Learning Spiral. You circle around through unconscious incompetence, conscious incompetence, conscious competence, unconscious competence, and by the time you arrive back over your starting position you are not at the same altitude. Your Matrix is evolved. You are not the same person.
