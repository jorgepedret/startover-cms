  • Elements Of First Position

    First Position is a wholeness. It is present. It happens all at once at the same time in the same place. As you.

    But you cannot effectively learn it that way.

    This would be like trying to learn Jiu Jitsu by fighting Bruce Lee. Aiii! You're down!

    Step-by-step preparations are the way.

    Get it together one step at a time and let each element of First Position naturally find its places as you can hold it....

    This is A Life Of Practice.

    First Position is a state of Being, not a state of doing.

    Below we list the core elements of a Possibilitator's First Position.

    A well-balanced natural-feeling First Position will take you a couple of years of hard work to become... of course!

    Once you become the harmony of these elements you will naturally jack-in to additional Resources......as a side effect.

    Then it starts flying all by itself.

    1

    BE CENTERED

    You have two centers. A physical center which is located 3 fingers below your bellybutton between your hipbones in the middle of you abdomen. It is your point of balance. And you have an energetic center which is moveable. When you give your energetic center away your give away your authority, your will, your feelings, your voice, your ability to ask questions, your ability to think freely, your presence, your commitment, your intention. To center yourself use your attention to locate your energetic center. It can be with other people, in the past or future, with a project or in your mind. Once you have located it use your intention to move your energetic center onto your physical center with the purpose of being centered.

    Staying centered requires carrying the experiential destinction so that your energetic body is sensitiv to moments when you are about to give your center away.

    2

    BE GROUNDED

    Then you use your power of Declaration to declare your grounding cord with your clicker which is one of your 13 tools. Your grounding cord is 5cm in diameter and connects your energetic center to the center of Gaia. Gaia is one of the Infinite Ressources available to you in First Position and you can communicate with her. Your grounding cord is a two way connection.

    3

    BE IN YOUR PERSONAL BUBBLE OF SPACE

    When you are centered and grounded it is time for you to declare your personal bubble of space. Your bubble is around you, from underneath your feet to above the top of your head. In your bubble is your space. Not anyone elses! No one can enter your bubble without your permission.

    4

    CREATE YOUR CUBE OF WORK SPACE

    Now that you hold space for your personal bubble of space it is time to create a cube of work space where ever you go. This cube includes you as the spaceholder and your partner if it is a relating space, your team if it is a meeting space, your car if it is a driving space, the person across from you if it is a cafe gettogether, your client if it is a coaching session. Basically in every space you move into you declare your golden cube of work space which is also grounded to the center of Gaia through a black grounding cord. Now the space has a purpose and you use your attention to hold space. You are no longer a victim of any space since you declare the space you want to work in.

    5

    CALL IN BRIGHT PRINCIPLES

    Every human being comes into this world carrying a unique set of 3-6 Bright Principles. They are facets of consciousness. Destilling your Bright Principles is done in a specific process with a spaceholder and a balanced set of Bright Priciples includes at least one Heart Principle, one Soul Principle and one Sword Principle.

    You can become the space through which these Bright Principles do their work in the world by calling them into your workspace with intention and clarity. These qualities of consciousnesss support the space noticeably.

    6

    HAVE YOUR SWORD IN HAND

    Destinctions are held by your being. Destinctions create Clarity in all of your five bodies. Clarity creates possibility. Your Sword of Clarity is your energetic tool for applying destinctions in reality. This cannot be understood by your mind. It is the experience of conscious application of anger at all times. Your Sword of Clarity is your capacity to sense destinctions in a space, to name them, piecing destinctions together into Inner Structure and also land destinctions. To be able to use your Sword of Clarity elegantly you first train your ability to navigate your attention, your attention on your attention, your presence and your ability to consciously feel angry. With your Sword at your side day and night and even most of the time drawn in front of you, you become visible in the world. You are now an active creator of life with precision and commitment instead of a mere follower.
