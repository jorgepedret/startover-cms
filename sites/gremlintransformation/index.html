What Is Gremlin?

  • Gremlin Transformation - The Process

    The point of transforming your Gremlin is to make what you receive available for others, in other words, to jack into the Flow.

    No one can transform your Gremlin for you.

    More interestingly, no one can stop you from Gremlin Transformation.

    After that, no one can stop you from providing Gremlin Transformation for others.

    Your next culture profession and Possibilitator Specialty might be Gremlin Transformer.

    The world is in need of Radically Responsible Possibilitators.

    Are you willing to become one?

    You start by sending your life into the Gremlin Transformation life-grinder.

    There is no clever way around this.

    You cannot 'understand' Gremlin Transformation and then deliver it for others. What a joke!

    The joke is on you.

    Once your Gremlin is transformed, the world will work differently for you, and everyone will see the difference.

    Before then... you have work to do.

    GREMLIN TRANSFORMATION IS A GATEWAY TO NEXT CULTURE: ARCHIARCHY

    Archiarchy is powered by skillsets and values that modern culture knows nothing about.

     

    Gremlin Transformation is one of those skillsets.

     

    Gremlin Transformation is evolutionary. It moves you (and your Gremlin) into entirely new domains of relating ecstatically, of creating originally, and inventing responsibly.

     

    The distinctions, tools, and processes in Gremlin Transformation are central to ANY Archiarchal adulthood initiation Path.

    Without Gremlin Transformation and its associated Healing and Initiation processes, you will not have enough experiential clarity, free attention, adult matrix, or freedom of movement to be Present without your Gremlin automatically running your life. You will only able to enact your automatic survival strategy.

     

    But you have already survived. Has the survival thing gone on long enough?

     

    Only you can decide.

     

    What lies beyond survival? Most people think the answer is: dying. But the surprising actual answer is: living.

     

    The untransformed Gremlin is dedicated to keeping you in survival.

     

    The transformed Gremlin is at your side as an ally - as an unlimited resource of nonlinear possibility - dedicated to you living full out.

    GREMLIN TRANSFORMATION OCCURS IN PHASES DURING SEVERAL YEARS OF FIERCELY PERSISTANT WORK IN A PRECIOUS GREMLIN TRANSFORMATION TEAM

    There are four or five Phases of Gremlin Transformation Work.

     

    The first Phase you will encounter is Phase 0. We call it 'Phase 0' because it is a piece of work which you cannot do for yourself. Someone else has to do it for you. Phase 0 of Gremlin Transformation is when someone tells you that you have an unconscious Gremlin that is destroying your life and eating your opportunities for intimacy and creation.

     

    Hearing this information is a nuissance, a bother. You may not even know what the speaker is talking about. In fact, the 'speaker' may be an author of an article or a book. They could be a panelist in a video, or a presenter at a conference. You did not expect to hear anything so devastating, insulting, or disgusting, yet something rings terribly true about what they say. You go into shock. You may hate this person for a while. You may run away as fast as you can and try to bury your head deep into the sand.

     

    On the other hand, some long-constricted knot in your heart may relax at finally being nailed dead center with a perfect hit. You have a Gremlin. It saved your life in childhood and adolescence. It gave you power and deviousness back then. It helped you to lie, and cheat, and steal your way through. It helped you to remain numb, hard, cold, tough enough to survive. It promised you 'sweet revenge', or very big excuses and reasons why your life had to be this way.

     

    And, eventually, as incident after accident piles on top of each other providing you with evidence about the existence, trickiness, and consequences of your Gremlin contaminating your Adult Egostate, blocking intimacy, and running your life with its addictions, secrets, and reactivity, you are ready to learn more. You are ready to face into the existence and unconscious purposes of your Gremlin. You are ready for something completely different from this. You are ready to wake up, change your life, and go down a different path than the one your Gremlin is taking you down.

     

    That is when you may find this website. That is when you may contact a Gremlin Transformation Spaceholder, call them up, and ask them, "Hey... I need you to tell me about Gremlin. Do I have a chance? Can I get out of this mess?" Then you are willing to listen.

     

    Someone has gone before you along this Path. Someone has gone first to discover or invent new options to choose from. This is when you need your Team. Making truly new choices is not simething human beings are famous for. Taking A Stand for new Clarity, new behavior, new Results is best done in a Team. Beypond this point you do not make it as a Lone Wolf or Single Fighter. You have too many grooves in your brain with years of habitual reactivity, years of habit patterns. Your Gremlin Transformation Team and 3Cell help each other catch the unconscious Gremlins at work keeping you small, dark inside, cold, and disconnected... keeping you in Survival when your soul is longing to serve something greater than yourself and to start living.

    GREMLIN TRANSFORMATION CHAPTERS (OR PHASES)

    PREFACE: GETTING READY

    GT Preface is about practicing the BINS specific to build the matrix required for someone to meet their Gremlin. The 5- body distinction of Gremlin is not necessary in the space yet.

    It is about experientially discovering what blocks Transformation - such as only living in verbal reality, pretending, making things right and wrong, being confused about your own defensiveness, constantly analyzing your experience and narrating it as it happens - and also practicing skills that enable Transformation - such as owning your attention, observing yourself Neutrally from the inside, navigating into experiential reality, identifying your own reactivity, discovering what you hide and revealing it.

    CHAPTER 0: MEET YOUR GREMLIN

    In Chapter 0, you get to know what Gremlin is and how your Gremlin has been working for your survival. Is it Type 1? Type 2? What are its favorite foods and usual behaviors? What is behind each one? Begin the Gremlin Food Research as a team and go through Initiations and Emotional Healing Processes to build a conscious relationship with your Gremlin.

    This is a journey of deep disillusionment, where you have the opportunity to get real with how exactly your Gremlin has been running your life, develop and clear practices to clearly and soberly see what Low Drama you create, so you can choose new more responsible options and actions that are aligned with your Love for life.

    CHAPTER 1: GREMLIN DIET

    A conscious Gremlin diet is for your relationship with your Gremlin and your underworld, what the gym is for an athlete. Your Gremlin has been eating your life energy, your intimacy, your integrity, and your access to your Bright Principles. You have been letting Gremlin take over your life. It's time for that to change. After having successfully completed Chapter 0, you have the clarity and the energy to make those changes. Chapter 1 gives you a space with high-clarity and integrity to create conscious discipline. Discover a path of practice and healing that can give you the tools for you to transform your life.

    CHAPTER 2: UNDERWORLD & TRANSFORMATION

    Chapter 2 is the bulk of Gremlin Transformation. Chapter 0 and 1 exist to build a conscious relationship with Gremlin: the biggest source of Non-Linear Creation in you. Without having a functioning relationship with your Gremlin, which means a relationship that you have the reins of, you cannot train it, you cannot transform it, and you will continue to have a persistent denying force inside you resisting your best efforts to evolve. Chapter 2 is about working with the Underworld, to all levels of it, and discovering what your Gremlin can do to empower your expansion, instead of your survival. You will discover how deep are some patterns in your Underworld; clarify and distill your Hidden Purpose sentence; explore the pitfalls and blindspots of navigating your Underworld; as well as the hidden powers and nonlinearity knacks of your particular Gremlin. It is here that you can optimally go through the Gremlin Reconstruction Process.

    CHAPTER 3: SERVING YOUR DESTINY

    Gremlin is a pure force of Non-Linear Creation that is part of you. You have been and still are developing a conscious relationship with your Gremlin, where your Gremlin responds to your calls, and you can make clear deals. Your Gremlin is ready to serve your life, creation and responsibility. You have your Gremlin at your side, fit, and eager to contribute in creating extraordinary and Archetypal moves for the unfoldment of your Possibilitator Specialty, your Bright Principles, and your Archetypal Lineage’s work in the world. Chapter 3 is about putting your Gremlin to work, giving it progressively larger High-Drama jobs, and applying its nonlinear skills — shifting identity, conscious Pirate disruption, resourcefulness, invention, and being unhookable — essential to spaceholding extraordinary and archetypal spaces. This is part of the Possibilitator Path.

    TRANSFORMING YOUR GREMLIN IS LIKE TRAINING A DOG (Your Gremlin is not your dog)

    I saw a man 'training his dog'.

    The procedure so exactly matches some parts of Gremlin Transformation. For example:

    • The leash.

    • The clarity and consistency of speech and agreements.
    • The reward with small bits of Gremlin / dog food chosen by the man, not by the Gremlin / dog.
    • The patience and persistance.
    • Avoiding the good / bad belief, judging, threatening, punishing, manipulation paradigm.
    • The simplicity and clarity of the requests from the man of the Gremlin / dog.
    • The repetition, repetition, repetition, to build new habits.
    • Creating a trusting collaboration that is good for the Gremlin / dog and good for the man.
    • Building an extraordinary new future for both of them working together.
    Before dog training, the dog only wants to be a dog: dig big holes, chase cats, get fed, get pats on the head, chew your shoes, bark at the door, sniff French Poodles and Basset Hounds, peeing on telephone poles.
     
    Before Gremlin Transformation, the Gremlin only wants to be an unconscious Gremlin: compete, be superior / inferior, be right, make wrong, blame, justify, complain, resent, hate, plot revenge, steal, sneak, lie, cheat, betray, drink, smoke, get addicted, not care, go insane, undermine intimacy, jab others with evil jokes, break hearts, be cynical, snide, and crass, laugh horribly at other people's pain, not care at all that anything dies. (To get graphic cinematic insights into the two sides of Gremlin, please watch Steven Spielberg's film, appropriately titled: Gremlins.)
     
    The only difference between dog training and Gremlin Transformation is that a Gremlin can actually relocate its Point Of Origin into a Transformed Gremlin context, with conscious and practical access to unlimited resources of Nonlinear and Unreasonable Possibility... a dog cannot do that.
     
    Gremlin can become a fierce wizard at your side, able to do anything your Box cannot do. This is inconceivably useful and High Level Fun!

    What Is The Purpose Of Gremlin Transformation?

    Gremlin Transformation is a dedicated path to initiate your Gremlin, meaning to transform yourself and your Gremlin in such a way that Gremlin stops serving your Survival, and serves conscious Creation instead.

    3

    What Is My 'Gremlin' Actually?

    Gremlin what we call the part of You that is your biggest source of Non-Linear Creation. It is part of your wit, your inventiveness, your cleverness. It is as if you were born with your own personal Genie, - like Aladdin's Genie from Middle Eastern folklore - , but instead of the Genie being imprisoned in a bottle and granting you 3 wishes, it is a part of you and is there to serve you until you die.

    4

    Is My Gremlin Bad?

    Gremlin is not good. Gremlin is not bad. Gremlin is Gremlin. Your left hand is not good, or bad, and it can help you slap someone or play the piano. Because most people in grew up in Modern Culture have not gone through authentic initiations that would change their purpose and orientation from Survival to Living, your Gremlin too (being a part of You) has not been initiated and is serving your current purpose: survival.

     

    Living as if Gremlin is bad is like living as if anything else is bad: you make it a rule, and everything that does not go with the rule you cast it out, reject it, repress it, kill it, destroy it, or hide it. If you make it bad, then you cannot face it and transform it. The same for good. If you make something good, then you cannot relate to reality, you are relating to the rule of what should be. Both Good and Bad are the same expression of a rule-base Shadow Principle.

    5

    How Do I Get Rid Of My Gremlin?

    You cannot get rid of your Gremlin. Ever. Considering getting rid of your Gremlin only happens when you think Gremlin is bad. You can get rid of your Gremlin as much as can get rid of your own head off your shoulders and still breathe in air into your lungs. Maybe you can take enough drugs that you can not feel the effects of the Low Drama that you are creating with the help of your Gremlin, but the Gremlin will still be there. Gremlin is a facet of you that has not been initiated.

    - scanning and handle other people's Gremlins;

    - being able to walk anywhere in the world and create out of nothing;

    - start new gameworlds that no one has ever thought about including yourself;

    - being able to create any space when it is needed at your will (and spaceholding skill);

    - being able to creatively collaborate with another wild and adventurous initiated adult;

    ...the list is infinite!

    Critical Media Analysis of Porn

    Critical media analysis tells us how the advertising and mass media industries use psychological tricks to encourage us to buy and spend more time watching screens. It works. The global entertainment and media industry is worth more than $2 trillion.

     

    However, few of us apply the same critical thinking to pornography. Like mass media, porn is big business. The size of the industry was estimated at roughly $15 billion in 2018 in the U.S. alone. That’s more revenue than Hollywood, Netflix, the NBA, Viacom, or the NFL.

     

    Porn influences individuals and our entire society. However, this influence is rarely interrogated. Porn production and use largely happens in the shadows. It’s mostly a private habit that people indulge in darkened bedrooms and offices. And unlike other large industries, it’s usually not discussed.     “Super-Normal” Stimulus

    Human sexuality is a powerful force, and is something that can be beautiful, enjoyable, and important. Pornography, in contrast, is a “super-normal” stimulus. Our ancestors did not evolve in a world where the internet provided instant access to explicit images and videos.

Porn, in contrast, provides a never-ending stream of digital images. This super-normal stimulus is why many men, and a smaller number of women, are addicted to pornography.

Porn also gravitates towards “shock content” as people become accustomed to hyper-normal stimulus. This is why porn so often contains blatant racism, real and faux underage and “teen” content, acts that are not actually pleasurable, BDSM, incest tropes, and outright violence. People who use porn don’t actually get anything out of it. They don’t get good sexual education. They don’t strengthen their relationships by building trust and intimacy. They don’t make money or become more enlightened. They don’t even get real, lasting pleasure (users consistently report feelings of shame, depression, and anxiety regarding their habit/addiction).     How to Stop Using Porn

    There are many ways to stop using porn, but the difficult part is the psychology of quitting. Unlike a drug like heroin or alcohol, porn is barely addictive at the physical level. People quitting heroin or alcohol often need medical support and gradual weaning. Porn is not like that. The addiction is primarily psychological.

    Most compulsive porn users are convinced that they need porn, that they like it, or that they’ll feel miserable without it—even though using porn makes them feel shameful and physically depleted. This is the psychology of addiction.

    So how can this be overcome? There are a few common methods used by millions of people around the world to stop using porn. These include EasyPeasy, Fortify, Your Brain on Porn, and others.

     

    1. Stop Using Porn with the Easy Peasy Method

    “Easy Peasy” is a method adapted from Allen Carr’s 1985 book The Easy Way to Stop Smoking. The method focuses on first transforming your attitude towards porn by seeing it as a drug that has no benefits and embracing a desire to quit. This method is interesting because it emphasizes happiness and contentment to be free of porn, and discourages people from feeling like they are depriving themselves of something by quitting. It involves reading a short book (also available as an audiobook) and is free.

     

    2. Stop Using Porn with the Fortify App

    Fortify is a smartphone and web app that is available for free to help people stop using porn. It was created by the people behind “Fight The New Drug,” an anti-porn organization. The app helps prompt you to take certain actions, get support from others, track your progress, and do mindfulness exercises. A free version is available, as is a paid plan with more tools that costs $10 USD per month.

     

    3. Stop Using Porn with Your Brain on Porn

    YBOP doesn’t prescribe a single program for quitting porn. Rather, it has a collection of tips and tricks collected over many years and from many people.

    Please note: these methods are not explicitly feminist creations; they are self-help methods that are largely apolitical. Many anti-porn communities and forums still contain misogyny. However, don’t let this discourage you from using the resources that are available.
