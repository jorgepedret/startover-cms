    WHAT IS TRANSFORMATION

    Transformation can be something you do, or something you experience.

    Verb Transitive: You Transform something into something else. You cause Transformation.

    Verb Intransitive: You are the thing Transforming from something into something else.

    What are the other things you can Transform into?

    Who decides?

    In other words, are you Transforming or are you being Transformed?

    LEARNING VS. TRANSFORMATION

    Learning has occurred when you have new ideas in your mind.

    Transformation has occurred when you can create new results.

     

    In Possibility Management, the structure that stands between you and your unlimited potential is recognized as a sophisticated survival strategy woven together out of Beliefs, Assumptions, Decision, Opinions, Stories, Expectations, Rules, etc. This structure is variously referred to as Personality, Ego, Worldview, Belief System, Comfort Zone, Identity, etc. Possibility Management refers to the conglomeration as the Box.

     

    The Box is a dead mechanism with the function of keeping you alive through your childhood and adolescence until you have built enough matrix in your Being to take hold of Adult level consciously responsible living. 'Living' occurs in an entirely different domain of experience than merely 'surviving'.

     

    The difficulty is that modern culture does not bring you through the transition from adolescence to adulthood. Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes (both Healing Processes and Transformational Processes). Initiatory processes were banished from the capitalist patriarchal empire some six-thousand years ago. Without a complete series of Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes you remain as a physically developed butterfly trapped in its chrysalis.

     

    Authentic Adulthood Initiatory Processes include examining identifications. For example, if I am identified with my Box as if my Box is me, (e.g. I am my beliefs, opinions, ego, etc.) then if the Box changes it feels like 'I' am dying. In the 'I am dying' story world, I will defend against Box changes with tooth and nail.

     

    Hmmm... 'tooth and nail...'? You have just met your Gremlin.

     

    Gremlin is that part of your Box which will do anything to keep things inside of you and outside of you the same, because these are the conditions under which your Box has been designed, so these are the conditions under which your Gremlin knows you can continue to survive.

     

    In short, what you are allowed to learn by your Box and Gremlin are whatever already agrees with what you already know and what your Gremlin things it can manage. Transformation is not allowed by an unconscious Gremlin serving its original mandate, that is, to keep you surviving.

    PHASE 1 OF EVOLUTION WORK

    For transformation to become possible, Phase 1 of evolution work must first be successful, which includes distinguishing your Being from your Box and Gremlin, wresting back your life from your Box's and Gremlin's agenda, and setting off in an entirely different direction from trying to learn more.

     

    Successfully doing Phase 1 Evolution work to prepare you for transformational initiations into adulthood is not a rule or a custom. It is not philosophy or religion. It is not protocol. It is the physics of transformation. It is how you enter the Path.

     

    By the end of Phase 1 Evolution work, your Box will be an optional toolbox available for your conscious use at any moment, your Gremlin will be well-fed and alertly sitting at your side on a short leash in your hand, ready to go nonlinear and invent possibilities whenever you give it that assignment.

    You don’t need to introduce everybody to everybody. People arrive with the question, "Where is my clan? Who has the same calling as me? Where are my true sisters and brothers in this work? I have been missing being with them for so long. Where are they?"

    People come to give away their life-long Treasure. That is their reward, that their Treasure can be given away and usefully used. They don't come to get something, but to give everything.

They have waited their whole lives for this opportunity to do their part, to creatively and effectively collaborate to bring their contribution to life.

Trying to orchestrate the event, trying to package the event or run the event as if the process understandable and reasonable is shooting yourself in the foot.

    Bringing in 'experts' and 'speakers' to consult the group is an insulting waste of time and loving energy. This is not an Instagram spectacle. This is creative transformation. Hello! The hell worlds lurk! The future of humanity on Earth is at stake.

Chaos is your ally in a Transformational Healing and Creation Space. Either you get chaos, or you get stiflingly insane rigidity that participants have to destroy to be free of it.
