  • What Is A Mind Machine?

    During childhood it is often necessary to disempower yourself so your parents don't leave you behind at a gas station...

     

    One effective way to disempower yourself is to install a 'Mind Machine' between your ears and your mind, or between your mind and your mouth.

     

    There are 9 standard Mind Machine designs that create a disempowering reaction or confuse you in various ways.

     

    If you are self-disempowered then you are no longer a threat, so then others do not have to 'kill' you. You eliminate yourself.

     

    This works until you become eighteen years old, or so, and a creative universe beckons you to step into its infinity field and unfold your adulthood potentials.

     

    You may have forgotten that you installed your Mind Machine, but that does not stop it from functioning perfectly. Your Mind Machine will keep you disempowered forever.

  • 9 Kinds Of Mind Machines

    "These are the only ones of which the news has come to Harvard.
    There may be many others but they haven't been discovered    ."

    - Lyrics from The Elements Song by Tom Lehrer

    1

    TIME DELAY

    Someone asks you a question and you do not respond. You don't even notice that they said something. Twenty seconds go by while their question pushes its way through heavy molasses in your Mind Machine trying to get to your brain. By the time you notice their question and start trying to think up an answer, they have gone on to something more lively, leaving you behind in the molasses... but you are safe.

    2

    BECOME CONFUSED

    No matter what someone asks you, if it is even 1% dangerous, you overwhelm yourself with thoughts so as to be confused. Your confusion is not about any particular thing that can be cleared up or answered. It is confusion for confusion's sake. You don't understand what they said, what they want, what their purpose is. Nothing is clear. Why would they ask this? Why did they ask you? You stand there in dumbfounded, speechless, deactivated confusion. They leave you behind, shaking their heads at your bewilderment as they go... but you are not harmed.

    3

    ENTER THE FOG

    Someone asks you a question or makes a comment that you should respond to and suddenly you are lost in an thick fog. You cannot hear yourself think. You cannot hear them repeat their question to you. All you hear is, MMMUUUUHHHHH... the Fog Horn. Everywhere you look is motionless, misty, cold grey fog. It does not go away... until the questioner goes away. After a while you shake your head. The Fog Horn stops. The Fog has cleared. You don't really know what has happened... but you are unharmed.

    4

    SWITCH QUESTIONS

    Someone asks you a question and you hear and answer a different question than was asked. You are sure that the question you answer is the question they asked. If it is not the question they asked, then it is the question they meant to ask. You would bet money that you are right. They repeat their question and you hear it differently again! Each time they ask your Mind Machine changes what you hear. People think you are an idiot, or perhaps just stupid. They leave you alone because it is no 'fun' for their Gremlin to torment you.

    5

    SLEEP WAVE GENERATOR

    Any time the conversation starts getting tense, like it did between Mommy and Daddy when Daddy came home late or Mommy drank or spent too much money. Your mind changes frequency, sinks into the middle of your stomach, and goes numbly blank. You don't move. You ooooze out sleepiness frequencies that hypnotize everyone. Anything sharp is dulled. Understanding vanishes in the space. People wander off and go do something else, never remembering what happened. You are safe. After awhile you move again.

    6

    COMPLEXIFY

    An uncontrolled impulse comes towards you and no matter what it is, your Mind Machine makes a mess of it. This is not confusion or doubt. It is hyper-clear hyper-complexity. Everything is connected so importantly to everything else, and you are SURE that this complexity MUST be understood and agreed upon by EVERYONE. Your Mind Machine spider-webs every imaginable detail of the situation out into so many directions and dimensions that people would have to spend lifetimes disentangling the mess. Not any part of the situation is simple to understand. Nothing is clear, so nothing changes. You are safe.

    7

    DATA DUMP

    If any conversation approaches something deeper than casual conversation, an alarm goes off in your Mind Machine that they might be trying to make you look stupid, or prove that you don't know, or embarrass, judge, or attack you. Your Mind Machine unleashes gigabytes of 'important' explanations out of your mouth. You drown the whole space in file-after-file of relevant data, reasons, proofs, explanation-after-explanation, until they forget the topic because they have to focus on not suffocating in your propaganda avalanche. You don't remember what you say. You cannot repeat yourself - that doesn't matter. What matters is that your data-dump crushes the life out of them. Nothing is figured out, but they get exhausted and leave you alone. You feel like you delivered a good explanation.

    8

    THE WIPER

    Someone asks you a question and your Mind Machine wipes the question away. Tabula Rasa! No problemo. You are not disturbed at all. You look at the questioner with a blank stare. Nothing got through and there is no residue. You hear the question through your ears, but before it can go into your mind, your Mind Machine vanishes it completely. You assume that you answered their question, but actually the question was vaporized by your Mind Machine. You are puzzled why the people around you are not satisfied. They give up and leave you alone. You don't really know why, but it does not matter because you are okay.

    9

    QUEEN OF THE PROM / SELF REFERENCER

    Someone asks you a question or things are happening around you. The scenario goes into your Mind Machine which extracts and vanishes anything from the question / situation that is not already understood in your Box's worldview. The Mind Machine removes any details that might confuse, overwhelm, or change you. The part of the question you understand is self-referenced. You answer it to your Box's satisfaction. The questioner has a different worldview and cannot make use of your answer. You do not care. The space gets navigated in a way that makes sense to you, but it does not go anywhere new or different. The space does not actually help people other than creating the illusion that you are 'holding space'. You remain untouched, but people around you are forced to regard you as 'out of touch with reality'. You remain harmless and unharmed.
