    Unplugging: When Nothing OpensEverything.

    Initiation into Archetypal Everything andNothing

     

    Definitions:

    TOUR: Tomb Of Utter Reverence – my inner place of rest, guidance andknowing. Where I am fully known in my energetic, emotional and Archetypal
    bodies.
    ARC: Archetypal Response Communication – way of communicating that opensspace for nothing and everything to meet.

     

    A beginning:

    “What are the steps?” I ask Him.
    "It's simple" he replies "You get a man and kill everything he is until
    there is nothing left”.

    Woman says: I realise I find the Nothing in the night. The Nothing to feel me, see
    the droplets I overlooked in daytime and numbed. The night is my reflection, off-load and
    down-load times.

    What if Icould experience that in relation with Man?

     

    Setting One: three Women and a Man

    Woman one to Man: “Who are you?”, she wanted to see if he buckled at the knees.“Meet me” her eyes commanded. Her Queen was assessing and carat rating – “Are you gold plated, or solid gold? Are you man or mouse? Have you got any balls?”

    He squirms. The space shifts yet does not hold.

    He follows his impulse.

    Man to Woman: “What is your real question, behind all those questions?”

    Game on!

    She squirms. He is evaluating the quality of her gold. The pupils of her Sorceress’s eyes dilated and She scans Him and wonders for a moment: “Is this a game of cat and mouse?”

    The attention of all three Women is on Man, the space holds, does not buckle. In the intensity, she can
    access a deep and wordless space.

    He finds the words: “I have to be a big enough Nothing for you”. It is a cue. The container is held, empty
    enough for Her next piece. One woman’s everything is unplugged, She can access parts of Herself she would normally keep hidden from men.

    The Women all felt it, experienced it - a totally empty space, different from any other offering.
    “What happened, What did you do?” She asks.
    “Nothing happened, I did notdo Anything”, He says.

     
    Notes:Women reflect on what the man is doing in the space. When there is nothing to
    reflect on they see themselves. Then the Everything can show up.

    A Woman will not bring the everything into form and share her experience when
    there are men who are something (not Nothing) in the space.

    "When you hold an empty space and don't fill it with you I can fill the space with me!"

    He says “I could not have done that with out you. Without your magic"

    NOTES FROM JULIA NEUMANN

    "Birthing Everything"

    “How are Everything and Nothing different? And how does it go, then, to be Everything with Nothing?” I asked.

    “That’s a great question for where you are at right now,” he said.

     

    [Gabriel: I (with no resistance) removed the question here that opened up Julia. If you are ready, the question potentially opens the birthing everything process. It preferably needs 2 spaceholders (1 Nothing, 1 Everything) in the same physical space.]

     

    My sadness swelled up in me. I had never considered this before. There was an emotional layer of it, a sadness of never having experienced that. Underneath, there lay a deep knowing; a longing to be just that, and of being held in just that, with no reflections back or interpretations or other encroachments and inputs into my space.

    “The steps to get there are clear and simple,” he said. “First, you’ve got to kill the man until he becomes Nothing. Then, you let your guard down and become Everything.”

    I realised how I had built a protection around my core to do exactly that: to protect the Everything that I am. Fear arose in me: How could I be without that? Because of my fear, I had gone and chopped away pieces of my men. Killing them only a little bit here and there. Retreating back into confusion. Back and forth; back and forth. I did not find Nothingness there.

    “What do you know about killing men in pieces?” she asked.

    My mother’s image appeared in front of me. Memories of her anger at my father; her dissatisfaction and frustration with him. Her nagging. Her fighting him. Inherited from a long line of women before her. My father did not die into the Nothingness; his Box did not crack open. Instead, he isolated. He took refuge in his head. He solidified there; a well-known strategy he knew from ever since he could remember – having taken refuge there before, from his mother chopping away on him and his father.

    It hurt, as a little girl witnessing all of that. There was no space for either: No space for my father’s Nothingness, his vast emptiness. No space for my mother’s Everythingness. What did I do with that? I withdrew. I built a protective shield around the area of my heart to my womb. In there, I hid my Everythingness.

    The shield was made of high amounts of fear. I found sadness there, too. And anger.

    It was made of dark, gooey energetic stuff. With high amounts of anger, I took it off, out of my system and vanishing it into a black hole. I removed the shield in an intense process with high intensity of emotions and feelings coming up, to the extent that I had sore muscles in my belly the next couple of days.

    I ended in a place of darkness. Slight green patterns in the dark. Fear. My eyes hurting. Trying to find out there, looking hard, what I had not allowed myself to see within.

    “You created that shield, that bubble,” she said. “You can change your mind and burst it.” That is what I did.

    Without this protective shield, I can sense more. I sense so much. Even right now, as I write. A man moving through the space, fast and loud with an aggressive energy. It sends shockwaves through my energetic body.

    I wanted to lie naked on the Earth. The impulses are immediate in that space of connection to Everything. I had been there before, and I did not have the words for it then. The impulses come from within and they are immediate. Direct. Clear. There is no confusion.

    In a space with other women an hour after my process, I could sense the lack of synchronicity between her mental, energetic and emotional bodies. I did not have to think about it; I sensed it. I noticed the disconnect viscerally in my system from the effect it had on me.

    It is such a precious experience, birthing my Everything. Coming home.

    I am staying in an Airbnb here in Costa Rica, on the property of the owner.


    I decided to hold space for the whole property (except his house) while I am here. One of the purpose of my spaceholding is caring for place. The caring here is easeful and ecstatic in comparison to the previous place I was staying.


    I wondered why, and this is what I figured out.
     

    In the previous place, the owners did not care about their place. Therefore, my own care was creating energetic war with their wish to not care. My purpose and their purpose were at war.


    However, where I am now, the owner loves his place and cares for it. My care supports his purpose. We share extraordinary intimacy, even if we do not speak or meet for days at a time. We are both caring for the same place. When I am holding space, then automatically I experience the Love I have for this place and the spaceholder.

    This is Magic.
     

    There are so many opportunity to experience Love, and it starts by consciously Cavitating, Holding and Navigating space.
