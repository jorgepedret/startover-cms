    Feelings And Pain

    So often, the best we are able to do in our life using Standard Human Intelligence Thoughtware (S.H.I.T.) about feelings, is to notice that we are feeling some kind of 'stress, tightness, or awful pain' around the heart area. Maybe you get a headache. Maybe you feel depressed, exhausted, or frustrated.

     

    It can be extremely uncomfortable, like a squeezing of the heart, like an ache deep in the chest.

     

    This 'pain' is the often the result of suppressing your Emotions.

     

    If you keep a high Numbness Bar and use the Old Thoughtmap of Feelings where anger, sadness, and fear are 'bad', 'dangerous', or 'negative', then when your Feelings or Emotions naturally arise, you train your Emotional Body to automatically suppress the information and energy. You may even have installed some kind of Survival Strategy to prevent you from expressing these 'embarrassing' or 'life-threatening' outbursts.

     

    It can be exhausting to exert so much energy to suppress huge emotions for so long. In addition to causing pain, suppressing Feelings and Emotions can also cause severe illnesses, and reactionary irrational behaviors.

     

    In Phase 1 of Possibility Management you can upgrade your Thoughtware, step out of Memetic Constructs, remove Energetic Blocks, and shift to the New Thoughtmap Of Feelings. You can learn Inner Navigation skills and discover your Inner Resources for Connection. You may experience a tremendous breakthrough to discover that what you have been experiencing as painful and labeling as 'pain' is actually suppressed Feelings and Emotions, extremely valuable inner resources.

     

    Upon realizing what was causing the confusion he had been carrying around all his life about the pain in this chest, one man said, "I want to thank you for what you said to me about pain: that it is suppressed emotion. This insight has given me possibility to build new relationship to pain and begin with new experiments with it. It really works! It helps me a lot to be more authentic. Now I know what to do when I feel pain and how to express it and live it. It also helps me to free myself from programs that I adopted to try to block me from this pain. Now I don't need this protection because now it is easier for me to live the pain, to accept it and express it as an emotion that it really is."

  • The Pursuit Of Happiness...

    by Dor Sharabi

    The pursuit of happiness derives from a story that the feeling of happiness is the only emotion that you should strive for all the time.

    Like eating sweets all the time, we try to look for that feeling in candles. After eating all the donuts in the world we're still stuck in search of it.

    When I hear someone say that he's trying to understand what makes him happy, I immediately ask what about angry? Don't you seek to know what will make you angrier? Or more fearful? Or sadder?

    Because, for example, now we need more Consciously Angry people! Fiercely angry at how we treat the Earth, furious at how we treat each other, and Radically Active that global warming is drying up the Dead Sea.

    We don't need more people looking for happiness, enlightenment and the next personal benefit from sitting in an endless lotus position. There are enough of people trying this. Happiness is already understood. The reason joy doesn't come, by the way, after countless attempts, is that it is impossible to really hold Conscious Joy without Conscious Anger, Conscious Sadness, and Conscious Fear. That would be Self Deception, Fantasy World, Child Ego State Contaminating the Adult Ego State. It is not transformative.

    Seeking only joy is like a child playing in Luna Park, eating sweet pink cotton candy all day long.

    We need more scared people, connected to their Conscious Fear, who can try enough new things carefully, and slowly go to the edge of where they have never been, and take one small step beyond what modern society has offered them.

    We need more Consciously Sad people, to connect with each other and take down all the guns and shields. Sad women and men who will share the pain of living in this time in intimacy with each other.

    We need sad communities that connect to the pain of Earth, and decide to change.

    When you have these 4 Feelings and 4 Emotions active together, happiness does not become something in the future you can aim for, but rather just one of the 4 sensations you feel at various intensities (from 1% intense to 100% intense) all the time that guides you along your Destiny.

    You are invited to open the door in the direction of the conscious power of your feelings . After that, you surf them.
