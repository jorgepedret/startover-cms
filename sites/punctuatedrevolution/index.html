      • To 'Punctuate' means 'to occur at intervals throughout (an area or period)', and, 'to interrupt or intersperse something with'.

         

        There are two ways to 'Punctuate' a void into a 'solid' thing, be it an object, or a system.

         

        One way is to take something away from that solid, to remove some of its substance, causing the system to become insubstantial.

         

        Another way is to Cavitate a bit of new emptiness or nothingness into the solid.

         

        Puncturing a solid with more and more holes eventually causes it to become porous enough to collapse under its own weight.

         

        In the graphic above, the worn-out washing-sponge represents modern culture - the capitalist Patriarchal empire - hollowed-out, weakened, toxic, nonfunctional, full of holes, ready to be tossed away. The holes are created when the source people - the geniuses - leave to go invent something more interesting and valuable.

         

        The bubbles represent many new Spaces, Cavitated by independent geniuses who are originating regenerative, adulthood-initiation-centered, nonmaterial-value, local-authority, Archiarchal cultures.

         

        A few of these new Spaces are Cavitated within the systems of modern culture, Recontexting those systems in new Values and Procedures. But most of the new Spaces are Cavitated beyond the edges of modern culture, thereby opening Possibilities that modern culture knows nothing about.

         

        Exiting geniuses puncture more and more irreparable holes in the fabric of modern culture until it can only collapse and sudden Paradigm Shift happens. Global society then changes from a controlled solid into an intelligent ecologically-directed cloud.

         

        No traditional form of 'revolution' is needed for Archiarchy to emerge. Archiarchy has already emerged through 'Punctuated Revolution'.

Your Box can be completely freaking out - Reacting and creating Reasons for everything - but You don't have to. You can remain completely Neutral and Present. Under your own Guardianship, your Genius is available to E.C.C.O., your Bright Principles, your Stellated Feelings Archetypes, and your Game Plan, and You can create what you came here to create as an Adult on the Path.

      • Punctuated Revolution

        The Status Quo defends itself like the Tar Baby. You may try to change the Status Quo by punching it in the face but your fist gets stuck in it up to your wrist. Punch it with your other hand and both fists are now stuck. Try to kick it, and soon enough you are so covered in black sticky tar you become indistinguishable from the Status Quo you were so committed to transform.

         

        The Status Quo of modern culture – as exhibited by it Memetic Patriarchal Virus takeover of the Human Morphogenetic Field of Earth – resists change, not through its capacity to control, as some would think, but through its strategy of changing the rules of engagement faster than anyone can actually master the game. We spend our time upgrading the software on our phone, laptop, tax forms, crypto currency trading platforms, etc. It takes our full attention just to stay in the game. The terror of being left behind – abandoned to the raw forces of nature unprotected by civilization – is a nightmare we cannot tolerate even for a moment.

         

        The net result is that you do not have enough time, energy, attention, will, desire, or money left over in your daily life (or even ‘on vacation’) to use for anything other than mere Survival. All your resources are spent on keeping up with the latest model of Prius and iPhone. You have no time to reconsider your true desires regarding whether or not you actually want to play in this game at all. If you let go the wheel, or turn your head for too long glancing down side alleys, you crash and burn in such totality there is no way to ever recover.

         

        An Edgeworker is someone who has discovered the actual limits of modern culture life, and rather than recoiling instantly from the fear of this discovery, stays at the edge. You simply stay there, waiting to see what you will Notice next. As your perceptions acclimatize and your views and perspectives become your new ‘normal’, two things become obvious. First: Modern culture is NOT all there is (not 'the best thing ever invented by human beings', as it is commonly marketed). And, second: Something completely different from this is possible right now. In other words, (r)evolution is possible.

         

        When an Edgeworker turns around and considers how to bring about valuable changes in the Status Quo, you are faced with the same problematical resistance as always, the Tar Baby awaits you. Modern humans are not initiated into Adulthood use of Conscious Anger, Conscious Fear, Conscious Sadness, and Conscious Joy. We remain Box-identified adolescents fearfully living in acquiescence and blind obedience, pretending to each other that we are ‘cool’ and ‘happy’.

         

        At first it seems as if only Edgeworkers activate their Potentials and Invent new worlds to live in. Yes, it works, and works marvelously! But to ordinary citizens, next culture remains invisible because it starts at the edges of modern culture, and ordinary citizens cannot imagine that modern culture has any edges at all.

         

        The results of 300 million Cultural Creatives around the world inventing and inhabiting Archiarchy during the past sixty years is that nearly nothing has changed in the modern culture status quo. Yet now, suddenly something is changing. Small groups of geniuses have escaped from a number of the 8 Prisons, and have learned to Cavitate and Inhabit New Culture Space. What this means is that changing the existing culture is no longer interesting, no longer on an Edgeworker's agenda. Instead, thousands of independent Parallel Cultural evolution Experiments are occurring simultaneously in every cultural corner you can imagine. Little to no effort is directed towards attacking or changing the existing gameworlds. Resisting the existing gameworlds is no longer necessary. Instead, Cultural Creatives have acquired Creative Authority and are aggressively and ecstatically Cavitating and inhabiting new culture space around the world, leaving modern culture behind like a butterfly leaves its chrysalis.

         

        Below the radar of modern culture – which does not have refined enough Distinctions to detect what is happening – modern culture has become more perforated than Swiss cheese. Modern culture is already drilled through with so many holes that it has become structurally unsound. A list of these holes would easily fill one-thousand pages. Modern culture is already hollow, and is unable to offer a bright future for humanity on Earth. What appears to be modern culture is now a handful of multinational corporations running modern culture news media, banking systems, politics, and the medical-military industrial complex, hoping to God that more slaves do not get bored, wake up, and go do something more interesting with their lives.

         

        The evolution of Consciousness is the most entertaining experience on Earth. It beats corporate dental and retirement benefits hands down!

         

        Here comes a prediction: The combination of increased social stress, such as floods, draught, political corruption, crop failure, lack of drinking water due to desertification, disappearing glaciers, and lowered water tables, etc., combined with the rapidly increasing number of cultural perforations – meaning increasing numbers of people creating a vacancy in society by leaving modern culture behind and Cavitating and inhabiting new culture space – will soon reach a cataclysmic spontaneous reordering point. When this happens, the 6000-year equilibrium of the capitalist patriarchal empire culture will suddenly collapse and reorder into Archiarchy without having been attacked from the outside, in the same way that the Berlin Wall collapsed and vanished in 1989. The long prevailing so-called ‘modern culture’ will perforate into chaos due to its lack of structural integrity. It will no longer hold its own weight or glue itself together by unconscious attraction forces because it is no longer as attractive as a culture that values and enhances each individual’s Nonmaterial Value and Awareness Potentials. Archiarchy will suddenly emerge in its full diverse dimensions through Punctuated Revolution.
