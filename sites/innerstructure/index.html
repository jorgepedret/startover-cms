    WHERE DID YOUR INNER STRUCTURE COME FROM ?

    Just because you can divide something up into identifiable component parts does not mean it was assembled out of those component parts. Let us not fool ourselves into thinking that there is some 'Architect' behind the curtain designing sensible systems that we can figure out and control. That is not the purpose of distinguishing and exploring your Inner Structure.

     

    The purpose of gaining practical awareness of your Inner Structure is to take Radical Responsibility for the consequences you are creating for yourself (and others...), and, from this orientation, to look into the heart of the matter - regardless of what degree of darkness or brokenness gets exposed - so as to gain the Possibility of Choosing in places where before 'things just happened' for reasons beyond your awareness.

     

    Human beings are holisms, just like the material world. We are complex interconnected systems, intimately intertwined with our:

    • past / present / future
    • external / interface / internal environments
    • physical / intellectual / emotional / energetic / archetypal bodies
    • upperworld / middleworld / underworld
    • biological / social / spiritual / destiny
    • conscious purpose / unconscious purpose (e.g. Hidden Purpose of your Gremlin, Hidden Competing Commitments, Old Decisions that remain active, etc.)

    In the middle of this mess, it is easy for you to imagine an apple, for example, but in reality, there is no such thing as an apple. (This is not meant to communicate what the 'Neo' character in the first Matrix film was told by the psychic-adept child to realize that: 'there is no spoon'. Perhaps we will get that far... but for now we are speaking plainly.)

     

    You fool yourself into imagining that there is 'one apple' because you can count it, name it, touch it, hide it, eat it, give it away, etc. However, the apple is merely one way of perceiving a system of four billion years of biological evolution on this very special planet we call Earth. The apple is one tiny part of an entire ecosystem of bacteria- and fungus-infested composting soils, communication exchanges between tree roots and mycelia, climate patterns, an orchard of well-bred apple trees visited by enough pollinators and insectivores that the trees can grow to maturity, not to mention the farmer... and her husband... and the transportation infrastructure of trucks, ships, cargo planes, and markets...

     

    There is no apple. There is a system out of which, from time to time, in certain circumstances, you can pluck and eat an apple. However, beware that you are merely a pawn in this game. With smells and colors and tastes, the apple genes easily manipulate you into carrying them to new spawning grounds. From the apple genes' perspective, you are merely the courier of it genetic material. In the end, the apple orchard uses you for compost to make more apples.

    The only useful answer to that question is that you made your Inner Structure yourself. You are the source of it.

    WHAT ARE YOUR OPTIONS FOR UPGRADING YOUR INNER STRUCTURE ?

    Your Inner Structure is an actual structure.

     

    For the most part, it is an energetic structure. But your Inner Structure has a shape. It has a form. More importantly, it has Reasons. You are easily able to justify or explain why your Inner Structure is like it is. You do this mostly to yourself. You have justified and explained why you are like you are to yourself so often that by now you believe your Reasons are true, that your Reasons are the cause. The reasons is not the cause.

     

    Why did you design your Inner Structure to be like it is right now? What circumstances were you in? How did the clever design of your Inner Structure allow you to survive this circumstance?

     

    After all these years of enduring the relational and causative consequences of your Inner Structure's particular shape, why have you not changed that shape before now? What is your benefit (and your Gremlin's benefit, and your mother's benefit...) of keeping your Inner Structure unchanged?

     

    What is stopping you from changing any of the parts of your Inner Structure right now? What is in the way?

     

    What would you require to make changes in the formation and function of your Inner Structure?

     

    What would you fear to change in your Inner Structure? What is the fear about, really?

     

    What would you grieve to lose about the form of your Inner Structure? What would you want to preserve at all costs? Why, really?

     

    What parts of your Inner Structure would you be very glad to change?

     

    What would insult you about having to change some part of your Inner Structure?

     

    Who has an Inner Structure that you would like to copy, at least parts of?

     

    Which character in which book or film would you like to model your upgraded Inner Structure on? What does their Inner Structure look like, in detail?

     

    What practices would show up to implement new behaviors from an upgraded Inner Structure?

     

    Who could hold space for your Emotional Healing Processes and respect you during the Liquid States of upgrading your Inner Structure?
