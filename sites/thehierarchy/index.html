    AVOID EVERY HEIRARCHY THAT YOU ENCOUNTER IN YOUR DAILY LIFE

    Matrix Code THEHIERA.01

    The purpose of this experiment is for you to notice how often you are engaging in the hierarchy without even knowing it.

     

    For 3 days, to avoid participating in any and every hierarchical interaction regardless if the hierarchy is formal or informal, written or unwritten

     

    For example, if you are working at a job in a company, and the job is a gameworld based on a written hierarchy, and you are probably not at the top, therefore, how can you do this experiment if you are going to the work?

     

    The answer is to constantly hold yourself outside of the hierarchy, what this means is having a metaconversation about the resources that you can bring in as an employee because you are functioning today outside of the hierarchy. For you, this may mean not being adaptive or playing small, staying quiet, not objecting, etc. Since you are working in the company and refusing to participate, start to look at every single person at the eye level.

     

    Another example, during these 3 days, there will be people trying to play lower than you in the hierarchy. For example, a bagger in the street, the lady at the cash registrar, or the grocery store. Or maybe people play a high status than you such as your mother, the police, a neighbor, a guard, or this man passing in the street.

     

    Remember this experiment is not participating in any hierarchy either higher than you or lower than you. In each case make a connection with the person as there is no hierarchy. While you are doing this experiment for 3 days, write down in your beep book every hierarchy you notice that is in your life
