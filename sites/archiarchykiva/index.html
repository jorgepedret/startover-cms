  • Archiarchy Kiva Team Context

    Kiva is a self-organized decentralized radically-responsible ubuntu-based private-banking cooperative-network (http://kiva.org). Kiva specializes in using your deposits to make custom-designed no-interest micro-loans to entrepreneurs all over the world.

    This means that any money you are not using can be used by others for free and then given back to you.

    Archiarchy is 'next culture', that is, the culture that naturally emerges now that Matriarchy and Patriarchy have run their course.

    Archiarchy plus Kiva is a perfect combination.

    We are evolutionary Teams helping each other incubate new projects in the Context of Radical Responsibility.

    Our Bright Principles are Love, Clarity, and Possibility.

    Archiarchy Kiva differs from traditional Kiva Teams in these ways:

    1. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses the Archiarchal context of Radical Responsibility.
    2. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses upgraded Archiarchal thoughtware from Possibility Management.
    3. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses Torus Technology processes for navigating circular meetings, governance, and decision making.
    4. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses The Purple Card and Conscious Feelings for Emotional Healing Processes and evolution.
    5. Archiarchy Kiva Team is centered on Transformation from authentic Adulthood Initiatory processes.
    6. Archiarchy Kiva Team uses distinctions, processes, and resources from Archiarchal Economics, such as Gaias and Becoming Money.
    7. We loan because we think that the opposite of scarcity is not abundance... we think that the opposite of scarcity is co-creating!
