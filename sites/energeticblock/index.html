    ARCHAN SHAMANISM

    There are two classifications of blocks that Shamans usually work with:

    • A block that comes from something being present that should not be there. This transformational procedure for getting rid of this extra element is known as 'banishment'.
    • A block that comes from something being missing that should be there. The transformational procedure for bringing this element back is known as 'retrieval'.

    The metaphor goes like this: There are two ways to get more hot water out of the shower. One way is to turn up the hot water. The other way is to turn down the cold water... Turning down the cold water can be a very efficient way to liberate more resources.

     

    Shamanism is a profession as old as humanity. Wherever and whenever something was/is not right, the Shaman's job is to bring things back into order and balance, assuring that individuals, families, and food supplies are healthy enough that the tribe can go on.

     

    'Archan Shamanism' is the kind of Shamanism that Archans do.

     

    Archan are from the culture of Archiarchy. Archiarchy is the culture that naturally emerges after Matriarchy and Patriarchy have run their course.

    Indigenious and Archan Shaminism are rooted in two different contexts.

     

    One notable difference is that Indigenious Shamans do their work on their Client whereas a Archan Shaman hold space for the Client to do the work on themselves.

    An Archan Shaman, you empower your client to remove the energetic block that they have put it themselves.
