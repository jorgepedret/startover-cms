    Riders are semiconscious energetic entities that can hang onto a human being either on the inside or the outside.

    There are numerous ways to detect and get rid of Riders. This section is not intended to be a complete reference about Riders. For more information, please consult the Curandero Tradition as delivered by Starr Fuentes in U.S.A., and, for example, Inge Koegel in Europe.

     

    A Rider has its own dietary needs and its own psychological neuroses that, if attached to a human, can interfere with the proper energy flow in a human body.

     

    The disturbance in the human energy flow over an extended timespan can manifest as various illness and discomforts for the carrier of the Rider, even including depression, tumors and cancer. The illness is caused by the human body naturally tries to get rid of the unnatural influence of the Rider.
